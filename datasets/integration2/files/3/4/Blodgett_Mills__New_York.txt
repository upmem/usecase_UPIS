'''Blodgett Mills''' is a [[hamlet (New York)|hamlet]] and [[census-designated place]] (CDP) in [[Cortland County, New York|Cortland County]], [[New York (state)|New York]], United States. The population was 303 at the 2010 census.<ref name="Census 2010">{{cite web| url=http://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/1600000US3606937| archive-url=https://archive.today/20200212192356/http://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/G001/1600000US3606937| url-status=dead| archive-date=February 12, 2020| title=Geographic Identifiers: 2010 Demographic Profile Data (G001): Blodgett Mills CDP, New York| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, American Factfinder| accessdate=December 17, 2014}}</ref> It is in the town of [[Cortlandville, New York|Cortlandville]], southeast of the city of [[Cortland, New York|Cortland]].

==Geography==
Blodgett Mills is located in the southeast part of Cortlandville and is bordered to the south by the town of [[Virgil, New York|Virgil]]. The [[Tioughnioga River]] forms the eastern edge of the CDP and flows south to the [[Chenango River]] and ultimately to the [[Susquehanna River]].

U.S. Route 11 passes just east of the community and is accessed by a bridge over the Tioughnioga. US-11 leads northwest {{convert|4|mi|0}} to the center of Cortland and south {{convert|10|mi|0}} to [[Marathon (village), New York|Marathon]]. [[Interstate 81]] runs just east of US-11 but is not directly accessible from Blodgett Mills.

According to the [[United States Census Bureau]], the Blodgett Mills CDP has a total area of {{convert|5.6|km2|disp=flip}}, all land.<ref name="Census 2010"/>
