'''Melis Özçiğdem''' (born October 26, 1982), aka '''Meliz Özçiğdem''', is a [[Turkish women in sports|Turkish]] [[FIFA International Referees List|FIFA listed]] [[Referee (association football)|football referee]] and former international [[association football|women's footballer]]. She is a teacher of physical education at a secondary school in [[Konak, İzmir]].<ref name="meb"/><ref name="kg1"/>

==Sports career==
===Player===
====Club====
Melis Özçiğdem began her sports career after obtaining her license for İzmir Öztürkspor in her hometown on November 8, 1999. She joined [[Ankara University]]'s football team on March 2, 2001 when she enrolled at the university in [[Ankara]]. On December 24 that year, she moved to [[Gazi Üniversitesispor]], where she played until the end of the 2001–02 season in the [[Turkish Women's First Football League|First League]] capping 8 times and scoring 10 goals. As the [[Women's football in Turkey|women's football league in Turkey]] was suspended between 2003 and 2006, she took a break. Finally, she transferred to [[Bucaspor Ladies Football Team|Bucaspor]] for the 2006–07 season.<ref name="tff0"/>

====International====
Özçiğdem was admitted to the [[Turkey women's national under-19 football team|Turkey U-19]] team and debuted in the [[2000 UEFA Women's Under-18 Championship Second qualifying round]] match against Ireland on October 19, 1999. She took part at the [[2001 UEFA Women's Under-18 Championship First qualifying round|2001 UEFA Women's Under-18 Championship First qualifying]] and [[2001 UEFA Women's Under-18 Championship Second qualifying round|Second qualifying]] round matches. She appeared in 11 matches of the Turkey women's U-19 team.<ref name="tff0"/>

On May 20, 2001, she played for the first time in the [[Turkey women's national football team|Turkey women's]] team at the friendly match against [[Greece women's national football team|Greece]]. Özçiğdem was part of the national team at the [[2003 FIFA Women's World Cup qualification (UEFA)|2003 FIFA World Cup qualification (UEFA) – Group 8]] and [[UEFA Women's Euro 2009 qualifying|UEFA Euro 2009 qualifying – Group A1]] matches. She capped 8 times in total for the Turkey women's nationals.<ref name="tff0"/>

She was part of the Turkey yeam at the [[Turkey at the 2005 Summer Universiade|2005 Summer Universiade]] held in İzmir.<ref name="us"/>

===Referee===
====Domestic====
Özçiğdem began her referee career as an [[assistant referee (association football)|assistant referee]] in a match of the Turkish youth league, ({{lang-tr|Deplasmanlı Süper Gençler Ligi, DSGL}}, on October 20, 2007. She was tasked in this capacity  at the [[Turkish Women's First Football League|Women's First League]] on November 30, 2008.<ref name="tff1"/>

She made her debut as a referee in the [[Turkish Women's Second Football League|Women's Second League]] on December 28, 2008. She served in various competitions of the Turkish men's [[reserve team]] league, called [[A2 Ligi]] from November 15, 2010 on. She was promoted to officiate in the [[Turkish Women's First Football League|Women's First League]] on December 5, 2010. She appeared for the first time as referee in the [[TFF Third League]] on September 20, 2014, and as [[Assistant referee (association football)#Fourth official|fourth official]] in the [[TFF Second League]] on December 14 the same year.<ref name="tff1"/>

====International====
;2013
Özçiğdem officiated two of the [[2014 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification#Group 4|2014 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification – Group 4]] matches in October 2013.<ref name="trt1"/>

;2014
She officiated two of the [[2014 UEFA Women's Under-19 Championship qualification#Group 6|2014 UEFA Women's Under-19 Championship qualification – Group 6]] matches in April 2014,<ref name="tffhgd"/> two games of the [[2014–15 UEFA Women's Champions League qualifying round#Group 6|2014–15 UEFA Women's Champions League qualifying round – Group 6]] on August 9–11, 2014 and two of the [[2015 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification#Group 1|2015 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification – Group 1]] matches in October 2014.<ref name="f1"/><ref name="tff2"/>

;2015
She served as referee in two matches of the [[2016 UEFA Women's Under-19 Championship qualification#Group 10|2016 UEFA Women's Under-19 Championship qualification – Group 10]] in September 2015.<ref name="uefa1"/><ref name="uefa2"/>

;2016
In 2016, Özçiğdem officiated the match [[Belgium women's national football team|Belgium]] against [[Estonia women's national football team|Estonia]] at the [[UEFA Women's Euro 2017 qualifying Group 7]] in [[Leuven]], Belgium on 12 April. She was assigned to officiate the [[2017 UEFA Women's Under-19 Championship qualification]] matches [[Norway women's national football team|Norway]] against [[Armeniaa women's national football team|Armenia]] in [[Plewiska]], Poland on 15 September, and between [[Armeniaa women's national football team|Armenia]], [[Poland women's national football team|Poland]] in [[Wronki]], Poland on 20 September. She served as referee at the [[2017 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification]] [[[[2017 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification]#Group 10|Group 10]] matches between [[Republic of Ireland women's national under-17 football team|Ireland]] and [[Belarus women's national football team|Belarus]] in [[Cork (city)|Cork]], Ireland pn 28 October and [[Iceland women's national football team|Iceland]] against [[Republic of Ireland women's national under-17 football team|Ireland]] in Cork, Ireland on 31 October.

;2017
Özçiğdem officiated the [[2017 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification#Ranking of third-placed teams|2017 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship qualification]] matches[[Slovenia women's national under-17 football team|Slovenia]] against [[Netherlands women's national under-17 football team|Netherlands]] in [[Zaltbommel]], Netherlands on 27 March and between [[Netherlands women's national under-17 football team|Netherlands]] and [[Switzerland women's national under-17 football team|Switzerland]] in Zaltbommel, Netherlands on 29 March 2017.  She served as referee at the [[2017 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship#Group B|2017 UEFA Women's Under-17 Championship Group B]] matches between [[Republic of Ireland women's national under-17 football team|Ireland]] and [[Norway women's national football team|Norway]] on 5 May, and between [[England women's national under-17 football team|England]] and [[Norway women's national football team|Norway]]  in [[Plzeň]], Czech Republic on 8 May 2017.

;2018
She officiated the [[2018 UEFA Women's Under-19 Championship#Group B|2018 UEFA Women's Under-19 Championship Group B]] match [[Germany women's national under-19 football team|Germany]] against [[Denmark women's national under-19 football team|Denmark]] in [[Biel/Bienne]], Switzerland on 18 July 2018. At the [[2017–18 UEFA Women's Champions League qualifying round]], she was the referee in the matches [[Stjarnan women's football|Stjarnan]] against [[ŽFK Istatov]] in [[Osijek]], Croatia on 25 August, and  between [[ŽFK Istatov]] and [[Klaksvíkar Ítróttarfelag (women)|Klaksvíkar Ítróttarfelag]] in [[Vinkovci]], Croatia pn 28 August 2018. In 2018, she served as referee ffurther in the [[2018–19 UEFA Women's Champions League knockout phase#Round of 32|Round of 32 match at the 2018–19 UEFA Women's Champions League knockout phase]] between [[AFC Ajax Vrouwen]] and [[AC Sparta Praha (women)|AC Sparta Praha]] in [[Duivendrecht]], Netherlands on 12 September.

;2019
She officiated the [[2019–20 UEFA Women's Champions League knockout phase#Round of 32|Round of 32 match at the 2019–20 UEFA Women's Champions League]] between [[VfL Wolfsburg (women)|VfL Wolfsburg]] and [[KFF Mitrovica]] in [[Wolfsburg]], Germany on 25 September 2019,<ref name="h1"/> and the [[UEFA Women's Euro 2021 qualifying]] competition [[UEFA Women's Euro 2021 qualifying Group F|Group F]] match between [[Hungary women's national football team|Hungary]] and [[Sweden women's national football team|Sweden]] in [[Miskolc]], Hungary on 4 October, as well as the [[UEFA Women's Euro 2021 qualifying Group C|Group C]] match between [[Norway women's national football team|Norway]] and [[Northern Ireland women's national football team|Northern Ireland]] in [[Stavanger]], Norway on 8 November 2019,<ref name="h2"/> Özçelik was the referee of the friendliy match between [[Turkey women's national football team|Turkey]] and [[Ukraine women's national football team|Ukraine]] in [[Menemen]], Turkey on 11 November 2019.<ref name="tff3"/>
