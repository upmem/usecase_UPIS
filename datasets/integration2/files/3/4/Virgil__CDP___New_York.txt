'''Virgil''' is a [[hamlet (New York)|hamlet]] and [[census-designated place]] (CDP) in the [[Virgil, New York|town of Virgil]], [[Cortland County, New York|Cortland County]], [[New York (state)|New York]], United States. The CDP was designated after the 2010 census, so no population figure is yet available.

==Geography==
The hamlet of Virgil is located in the west-central part of the town of Virgil, in the valley of Virgil Creek, a west-flowing stream which is part of the [[Cayuga Lake]] watershed. New York State Routes [[New York State Route 392|392]] and [[New York State Route 215|215]] intersect at the center of the hamlet. Route 392 leads east {{convert|7|mi|0}} to [[U.S. Route 11]] in Messengerville, and west {{convert|6|mi|0}} to [[New York State Route 13|NY Route 13]] in the village of [[Dryden (village), New York|Dryden]], while Route 215 leads north {{convert|6|mi|0}} to [[Cortland, New York|Cortland]], the [[county seat]].

According to the [[United States Census Bureau]], the Virgil CDP has a total area of {{convert|2.3|km2|disp=flip}}, all land.<ref>{{Cite web| url=http://www2.census.gov/geo/gazetteer/2014_Gazetteer/2014_gaz_place_36.txt| title=2014 U.S. Gazetteer Files: Places: New York| publisher=U.S. Census Bureau, Geography Division| accessdate=December 17, 2014| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141213014810/http://www2.census.gov/geo/gazetteer/2014_Gazetteer/2014_gaz_place_36.txt| archive-date=December 13, 2014| url-status=dead| df=mdy-all}}</ref>
