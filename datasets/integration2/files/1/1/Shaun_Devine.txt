'''Shaun Devine''' is an Australian former professional [[rugby league]] footballer who played professionally for the [[Western Suburbs Magpies]] and the [[Western Reds]].

==Playing career==
Devine played for the [[Western Suburbs Magpies]] between 1990 and 1993 in the [[NSWRL Premiership]]. In 1994 he played for the Rockingham Coastal Sharks in the [[Western Australia Rugby League]] competition.<ref>[http://www.sportingpulse.com/club_info.cgi?client=1-2127-19778-0-0&sID=20882&&news_task=DETAIL&articleID=54897 Rockingham Rugby League Honour Board] ''Rockingham Coastal Sharks''</ref> He then played for the new [[Western Reds]] franchise between 1995 and 1997.<ref>[https://www.independent.co.uk/sport/rugby-league-paris-surrender-galls-goodway-1252912.html Paris surrender galls Goodway] ''The Independent'', 28 July 1997</ref>

In 2008 he was involved in the formation of the Hinterland Storm club in the Bycroft Cup on the Gold Coast.<ref>[http://www.goldcoast.com.au/article/2008/11/14/22771_gold-coast-sport.html Matt to play on to help out pal] ''goldcoast.com.au'', 14 November 2008</ref>
