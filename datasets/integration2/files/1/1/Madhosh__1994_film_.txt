'''''Madhosh''''' is a [[Bollywood films of 1994|1994]] [[India]]n film directed by [[Vikram Bhatt]] and produced by [[Tahir Hussain]]. The film is Tahir Hussain's son [[Faisal Khan]]'s debut as a leading actor, having previously played bit parts in his brother [[Aamir Khan]]'s films ''[[Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak]]'' and ''[[Jo Jeeta Wohi Sikandar]]''. The film also stars Anjali Jathar in her film debut, [[Kiran Kumar]], [[Dilip Dhawan]] and [[Supriya Pathak]]. The film bombed at the box-office, but was appreciated for its musical score.

==Soundtrack==
[[Anand-Milind]] scored the music while [[Sameer (lyricist)|Sameer]] authored the lyrics. The music wasn't successful, but is considered one of the best musical scores from the duo.
