'''''Madhosh''''' is a [[Bollywood films of 1951|1951]] Bollywood film. The story was loosely based on [[Romeo and Juliet]].

== Cast ==
* [[Manher Desai]]  :  Raya, the hero
* [[Meena Kumari]] (as Manhar) :  Soni, the heroine
* [[Usha Kiran]]  :  Raina, a girl who loves Raya
* [[Rajan (actor)|Rajan]]  :  Anand, who Soni's parents want her to marry
* [[Kuldeep Akhtar]]  :  Rambhaji, Raya's father
* [[Mubarak]]  :  Bhujba or Bhujwa, Soni's father
* [[Jilloo Maa]] (as Jiloo) :  Soni's mother and Bhujba's wife
* [[S. Nazir]]  :  Shakriya
* [[Habib Sandow]]  :  Pahadji
* [[Shivdutt]]  :  Vamia
* [[Bismillah (actor)|Bismillah]] (as Bismilla) :  Narayan
* [[Ramakrishna]]  :  Panditji (as Ram Krishan)
* Goldstein  :  Police Superintendent
