'''Yoshihiro Suzuki''' (鈴木 義広, born January 5, 1983 in [[Mannō, Kagawa|Mannō]], [[Kagawa Prefecture]]) is a retired [[Japanese people|Japanese]] professional [[baseball]] [[pitcher]]. He played for the [[Chunichi Dragons]] in [[Japan]]'s [[Nippon Professional Baseball]] for the entirety of his career.

Since 2015, he has been employed as the Dragons scorer.
