"'''Friends'''" is a song by [[Danish people|Danish]] singer-songwriter [[Aura Dione]] from her second studio album, ''[[Before the Dinosaurs (album)|Before the Dinosaurs]]''. The song features American production team [[Rock Mafia]] who co-wrote the song with Dione and [[David Jost]]. Dione wrote the song to let her friends know how precious they are to her. "Friends" was released as the album's second single on March 2, 2012. It became Dione's third top ten hit in her native Denmark, where it peaked at number six. "Friends" also became a top ten hit in Germany, Switzerland and Austria.

== Chart performance ==
The song debuted at number twenty-five on February 17, 2012 in Denmark.<ref>{{cite web|title=Track Top-40|url=http://hitlisterne.dk/default.asp?w=06&y=2012&list=t40|publisher=[[Tracklisten]]. IFPI Danmark & Nielsen Music|accessdate=February 17, 2012|date=February 17, 2012}}</ref> The following week the song rose to number twelve.<ref>{{cite web|title=Track Top-40|url=http://hitlisterne.dk/default.asp?w=07&y=2012&list=t40|publisher=[[Tracklisten]]. IFPI Danmark & Nielsen Music|accessdate=February 24, 2012|date=February 24, 2012}}</ref> In its third week on the chart the song peaked at number six, becoming Dione's third top 10 single.<ref name="DEN"/> The song has since been certified gold by [[IFPI Denmark]] for sales of 15,000 digital copies. In Switzerland, "Friends" debuted at number sixty-four on the chart issue dated February 26, 2012.<ref name="SWI"/> In its fourth week on the chart, the song peaked at number ten on March 18, 2012.<ref name="SWI"/> The song has since been certified gold by [[International Federation of the Phonographic Industry]] (IFPI) for shipments of 15,000 copies in Switzerland. "Friends" debuted at number thirteen in Austria on the chart dated March 9, 2012.<ref name="AUT"/> The following week it peaked at number three.<ref name="AUT"/> The song debuted at number five in Germany, becoming Dione's third top 10 hit.<ref name="GER"/> On the chart issue dated May 25, 2012 the song hit number 1 on [[ZPAV|Polish Airplay Top 5]].

== Live performances ==
Aura Dione performed "Friends" on November 30, 2011 on German television show ''[[The Dome (television program)|The Dome]]'' along with "[[Geronimo (Aura Dione song)|Geronimo]]". On February 14, 2012 Aura performed the song in Moscow, Russia on ''Big Love Show''. On February 18, 2012 she performed the song for the first time on Danish television in the singing competition ''[[Voice – Danmarks største stemme|Voice – The Biggest Voice of Denmark]]'' together with the eight semi-finalists.
