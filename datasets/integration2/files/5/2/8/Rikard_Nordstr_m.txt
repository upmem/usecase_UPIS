'''Rikard Hannibal Wilhelm Nordstrøm''' (April 23, 1893 - February 7, 1955) was a [[Denmark|Danish]] [[gymnast]] who competed in the [[1912 Summer Olympics]].

He was part of the Danish team, which won the bronze medal in the gymnastics men's team, free system event.
