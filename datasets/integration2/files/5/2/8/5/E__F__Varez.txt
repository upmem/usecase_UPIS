'''Emmanuel François Varez''' called '''E. F. Varez''' (17 October 1780 – 8 September 1866) was an early 19th-century French playwright and novelist.

Born in the [[1st arrondissement of Paris]], Varez was a [[dramaturge]] at the [[Théâtre de l'Ambigu-Comique]] and the [[Théâtre de la Gaîté (boulevard du Temple)|Théâtre de la Gaîté]] and his [[play (theater)|plays]] were presented, with some exceptions, on the stages of these two theatres.

He died in the [[16th arrondissement of Paris]].
