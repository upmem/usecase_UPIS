'''Lamplight Analytics''' is a [[social media analytics]] company headquartered in [[Hong Kong]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.silicondragonventures.com/featured/10-startups-up-for-awards-at-silicon-dragon/|title=10 Startups Up For Awards At Silicon Dragon |publisher=Silicon Dragon Ventures|access-date=2016-05-20}}</ref> The company provides a [[Software as a service|software-as-a-service]] ([[SaaS]]) social media platform called Lamplight that allows users to capture and aggregate data from [[social media]], [[blog]]s, boards, [[Internet forum|forums]] and other [[Digital media|media online]].

Lamplight further interprets social media data using proprietary technology, [[algorithm]]s, [[machine learning]] and [[natural language processing]] so businesses and individuals can understand the world's social media data. The tool offers [[Data visualization|data visualisation]], [[sentiment analysis]], influencer identification and [[geolocation]], among other features that help users uncover important insights through social media data quickly and easily.

== History ==
Lamplight Analytics was launched in November 2014.

The company's current management includes Fergus Clarke, former Head of Research in [[North Asia]] for Edelman Intelligence,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.campaignasia.com/Article/355214,Edelman+launches+research+and+analytics+unit+in+Greater+China.aspx|title=Edelman launches research and analytics unit in Greater China|website=Campaign Asia-Pacific|access-date=2016-05-20}}</ref> the research, analytics and measurement division of [[Edelman (firm)|Edelman]] and Nathan Pacey, former Technical Architect at [[SAI Global]] and Head of IT and Risk at [https://www.aus61business.com/company/Arnott-Capital-Pty-Ltd Arnott Capital], where he worked on [[high-frequency trading]] platforms, [[Data warehouse|data warehousing]] strategy and implementation.

== Business Model ==
Lamplight Analytics primarily offers a [[freemium]] SaaS product (called Lamplight) to its users. The company also provides a suite of added [[Enterprise software|enterprise-level]] services including reporting, data & platform integration, influencer identification, customization and consulting services.

=== Funding ===
In September 2015, Lamplight Analytics raised US$1.5 million in [[seed funding]]<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.techinasia.com/lamplight-social-media-analytics-funding|title=Tech in Asia - Connecting Asia's startup ecosystem|website=www.techinasia.com|language=en-US|access-date=2016-05-20}}</ref> with the round led by Vectr Ventures.<ref name=":0" />
