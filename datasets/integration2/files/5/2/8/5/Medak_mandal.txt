'''Medak mandal''' is one of the 46 [[mandal]]s in [[Medak district]] of the [[Indian state]] of [[Telangana]]. It is under the administration of [[Medak revenue division]] and the headquarters are located at [[Medak]].<ref name=census>{{cite web|title=District Census Handbook – Medak|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/dchb/2804_PART_B_DCHB_MEDAK.pdf|website=Census of India|accessdate=20 May 2016|pages=12,136|format=PDF}}</ref><ref name=map>{{cite web|title=Medak District Mandals|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/maps/atlas/28part3.pdf|publisher=Census of India|accessdate=19 January 2015|pages=50,110|format=PDF}}</ref> The mandal is bounded by [[Ramayampet]], Shankarapet (R), [[Kulcharam]], [[Papannapet]] mandals and  a portion of it also borders [[Nizamabad district]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Mandals in Medak district|url=http://www.aponline.gov.in/quick%20links/apfactfile/info%20on%20districts/Medak.html|publisher=aponline.gov.in|accessdate=28 July 2014|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140210115127/http://www.aponline.gov.in/quick%20links/apfactfile/info%20on%20districts/medak.html|archivedate=10 February 2014}}</ref>

== Government and politics ==

Medak mandal is one of the [[List of constituencies of Telangana Legislative Assembly#Medak district constituencies|four mandals]] under [[Medak (Assembly constituency)|Medak assembly constituency]], which in turn represents [[Medak (Lok Sabha constituency)|Medak lok sabha constituency]] of [[Telangana Legislative Assembly]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Delimitation of Parliamentary and Assembly Constituencies Order, 2008 |url=http://eci.nic.in/eci_main/CurrentElections/CONSOLIDATED_ORDER%20_ECI%20.pdf
|publisher=Election Commission of India|accessdate=21 May 2016|page=10,29|format=pdf}}</ref>
