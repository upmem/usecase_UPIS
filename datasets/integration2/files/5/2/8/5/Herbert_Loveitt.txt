'''Herbert Loveitt''' (8 March 1874 &ndash; 18 February 1909) was a [[Great Britain|British]] [[rugby union]] player. He competed at the [[1900 Summer Olympics]] and won [[olympic medal|silver]] as part of the Great Britain team in what was the [[Rugby union at the 1900 Summer Olympics|first rugby union competition]] at an [[Olympic Games]].<ref name="SR" />
