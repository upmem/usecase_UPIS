'''Muntirayuq''' ([[Quechua language|Quechua]] ''muntira'' an ancient cloth cap; a bullfighter's hat, ''-yuq'' a suffix,<ref name=pirwa/><ref name=laime/> 'the one with a cap', also spelled ''Monterrayoc'') is a mountain in the [[Andes]] of [[Peru]] which reaches a height of approximately {{convert|4600|m|ft|0}}. It is located in the [[Junín Region]], [[Jauja Province]], [[Apata District]].<ref name=escale/>
