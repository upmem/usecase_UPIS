'''Victor Larchandet''' (29 December 1863 &ndash; 8 November 1936) was a French [[rugby union]] player. He competed at the [[1900 Summer Olympics]] and won [[olympic medal|gold]] as part of the French team in what was the [[Rugby union at the 1900 Summer Olympics|first rugby union competition]] at an [[Olympic Games]].<ref name="olympic stats">{{cite web | url=http://www.games-encyclo.org/index.php?id=33181&L=1 | title=Olympic Facts and Results | publisher=Encyclopedia of the Games | accessdate=20 May 2016}}</ref>
