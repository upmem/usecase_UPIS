'''''Herb Alpert / Hugh Masekela''''' is collaborative studio album by [[Herb Alpert]] and [[Hugh Masekela]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Hugh Masekela - Biography|url=https://www.amoeba.com/hugh-masekela/artist/116209/bio|website=[[Amoeba Music]]|publisher=amoeba.com|accessdate=13 July 2017}}</ref> It was recorded in Hollywood, California and released in 1978 via [[A&M Records]] and [[Horizon Records]] labels.<ref>{{cite web|title=Herb Alpert / Hugh Masekela – Herb Alpert / Hugh Masekela |url=https://www.discogs.com/Herb-Alpert-Hugh-Masekela-Herb-Alpert-Hugh-Masekela/release/712123|website=[[Discogs]]|publisher=discogs.com|accessdate=20 May 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=DISCOGRAPHY: 1970-1979|url=http://www.dougpayne.com/hmd7079.htm#RECONSTRUCTION|publisher=dougpayne.com|accessdate=20 May 2016}}</ref>
