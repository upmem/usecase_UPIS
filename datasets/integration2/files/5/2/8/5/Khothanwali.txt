'''Khothanwali''' is a village in [[Hanumangarh]] district of [[Rajasthan]], [[India]]. It belongs to Bikaner division. It is 48&nbsp;km west of Hanumangarh. Suratgarh is the nearest railway station. It is 3&nbsp;km from National Highway NH-15 (62)and 440&nbsp;km from state capital [[Jaipur]]. In 2008 awarded the Nirmal Gram.

==Education==
The village has two government Higher School separately for boys and girls, one co-education school, one Middle and one secondary school. There are a number of non Government Middle and Secondary Schools.

* Govt. Sr. Secondary School
* Balaji Higher Primary School
* Swami vivekanand Higher Primary School

==Hospitals==
* Govt. deputy Health Centre

==Chemists and Laboratories==
More than a dozone Chemists and 1 Laboratories fulfills primary medical needs for the village
 
==Language==
Bagri is the major language in Hanumangarh.{{Citation needed|date=May 2016}} It is also known as Khichdi language because of having Punjabi words and a Haryanvi accent. Punjabi language is also used as a second language of area and as the predominant language in northern areas along the border of Punjab. Hindi is the state language and English is also used among officials and children.

==Economy==
The economy of the Village is based on grain market & agriculture, its main crops are wheat, mustard and cotton. Other crops are guar, bajra, sugar cane and grams. In recent years, farmers are also diverting towards horticulture. Kinnow(a citrus family fruit or a hybrid citrus fruit from [[Orange (fruit)|Orange]]) is a popular horticultural product; other fruits of the citrus family are also grown.
