'''Wenche Kjølås''' (born 20 December 1962) is a [[Norwegia]]n businessperson.

She hails from [[Stranda]],<ref name=bt1jobb>{{cite news|title=Min første jobb|date=13 August 2011|work=Bergens Tidende|language=Norwegian}}</ref> and is educated as a master in economics and business administration from the [[Norwegian School of Economics]].<ref name=dof>{{cite web|url=http://www.dof.no/en-GB/About-DOF-ASA/The-Board/Wenche-Kj%C3%B8l%C3%A5s.aspx|title=Wenche Kjølås|publisher=DOF ASA|accessdate=24 June 2014}}</ref> From 1986 to 1992 she worked in [[Touche Ross]]. She then became financial manager in [[Hakon Gruppen]] from 1993 to 1995 and financial director in [[Kavli Holding]] from 1995.<ref name=bt1jobb/> She was also managing director in [[Kavli]] from 1997 to 1999

From 2006 she was the [[chief financial officer]] of [[Grieg Logistics]],<ref name=bt1jobb/> and in 2009 she became managing director of Grieg Maturitas.<ref name=dof/> This is the parent company in Grieg Gruppen,<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bt.no/nyheter/okonomi/Bedre-enn-ventet-for-Grieg-2885735.html#.U6ko6rG7bIU|title=Bedre enn ventet for Grieg|last=Gaasemyr|first=Magne|date=25 April 2013|work=Bergens Tidende|language=Norwegian|accessdate=24 June 2014}}</ref> and Kjølås is also a board member of the daughter companies [[Grieg Group Resources]] (chair), Grieg Seafood, Grieg Property, Grieg Logistics and Grieg Gaarden.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://e24.no/bedrift/rolle/508045/wenche-kjoelaas/|title=Wenche Kjølås|publisher=[[E24 Næringsliv|E24 Bedrift]]|language=Norwegian|accessdate=24 June 2014}}</ref>

In 1996–1997 Kjølås was a member of the governmental [[Norges Eksportråd]].<ref name=nsd>{{cite web|url=http://www.nsd.uib.no/polsys/data/forvaltning/utvalg/person/28734|title=Wenche Kjølås|publisher=Norwegian Social Science Data Services (NSD)|language=Norwegian|accessdate=24 June 2014}}</ref> In 2006 she was noted as being among the most sought-after female board members in Norwegian enterprise, with six memberships on boards of directors.<ref>{{cite news|title=Populære styrekvinner|date=4 June 2006|work=Dagbladet|page=10|language=Norwegian}}</ref> Kjølås chairs the board of [[Flytoget]] since 2013,<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bt.no/nyheter/valg/Har-fatt-inn-syv-flere-vestlendinger-2946317.html?xtor=RSS-2#.U6iSXrG7bIU|title=Har fått inn syv flere vestlendinger|last=Holm|first=Marit|date=13 August 2013|work=Bergens Tidende|language=Norwegian|accessdate=23 June 2014}}</ref> and has been a member of the board of [[Cermaq]] (2003–2009), [[Selvaag Bolig]] (2011–2013),<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.griegseafood.no/default.aspx?pageId=131|title=Styret|publisher=Grieg Seafood|language=Norwegian|accessdate=24 June 2014}}</ref> [[DOF ASA]],<ref name=dof/> [[Petroleum Geo-Services]], [[Kavli]] and [[Q–meieriene]].
