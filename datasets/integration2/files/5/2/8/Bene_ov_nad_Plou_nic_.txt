'''Benešov nad Ploučnicí''' ({{lang-de|Bensen}}) is a town in the [[Ústí nad Labem Region]] of the [[Czech Republic]]. It has about 3,700 inhabitants.

A castle in Saxon Renaissance<ref>Art et Histoire de la Bohême, p.127, edited in 2007 by Casa Editrice Bobechi, Italy</ref> style closes the main square.

==Notable people==
*[[Kurt Pscherer]] (1915–2000), Austrian theatre director
*[[Roland Ducke]] (1934–2005), German footballer
*[[Peter Ducke]] (born 1941), German footballer
