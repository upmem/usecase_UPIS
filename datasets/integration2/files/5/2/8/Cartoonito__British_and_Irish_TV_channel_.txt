'''Cartoonito''' is a British [[television channel]] which targets children between the ages of 2 and 6.<ref name="demographic">{{Cite web |url=https://www.awn.com/news/turner-broadcasting-launch-cartoonito-across-emea |title=Turner Broadcasting To Launch Cartoonito Across EMEA |last=DeMott |first=Rick |date=21 April 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190808065459/https://www.awn.com/news/turner-broadcasting-launch-cartoonito-across-emea |archive-date=8 August 2019 |access-date=17 November 2019}}</ref> It is run by [[AT&T]]'s [[WarnerMedia]] under its international division.

==History==
Cartoonito began as a programming block on [[Cartoon Network Too]] on 4 September 2006, running from 6 AM to 3 PM. It later expanded into its own channel on 24 May 2007, now running between 3 AM and 7 PM in a time-sharing agreement with [[Turner Classic Movies (UK & Ireland)#TCM 2|Turner Classic Movies 2]] (which later became Turner Classic Movies +1 after 13 August 2013).{{citation needed|date=August 2020}}

On 25 March 2010, Cartoonito was added to Virgin Media.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pressoffice.virginmedia.com/phoenix.zhtml?c=205406&p=irol-newsArticle&ID=1396640&highlight=|title=Virgin Media Expands Kids' Line-up with Cartoonito|publisher=Virgin Media|date=1 March 2010}}{{dead link|date=July 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> In early 2011, the channel's runtime was changed, now running from 4 AM to 8 PM. On 15 December 2016, it was added to [[TalkTalk TV|TalkTalk]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Cartoonito UK Now Available On TalkTalk TV|url=https://www.regularcapital.com/2016/12/cartoonito-uk-now-available-on-talktalk-tv/|website=Regular Capital|accessdate=19 April 2018}}</ref>

Cartoonito shifted its aspect ratio to 16:9 widescreen on 1 November 2017. The channel moved to a full 24-hour schedule on 15 January 2018.<ref>{{cite web|title=Cartoonito UK Now Available 24 Hours A Day|url=https://www.regularcapital.com/2018/01/cartoonito-uk-now-available-24-hours-day/|website=Regular Capital|accessdate=26 January 2018}}</ref>

== Mascots ==
Lolly (Purple Cylinder), Ringo (Green Ring) and Cuba (Red Cube) are voiced by [[Maria Darling]] while Spike (Blue Pyramid), Ting (Pink Star) and Bubble (Yellow Sphere) are voiced by [[Shelley Longworth]].
