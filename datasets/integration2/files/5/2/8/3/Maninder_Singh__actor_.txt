'''Maninder Singh''' is an Indian actor. He played Indian independence activist [[Ananta Singh]] in the film ''[[Khelein Hum Jee Jaan Sey]]'' (2010).<ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.livemint.com/Leisure/Q75PX6l2Tc6np8iUywnjcN/Film-Review--Khelein-hum-jee-jaan-se.html | title=Film Review: Khelein Hum Jee Jaan Sey | newspaper=[[Live Mint]] | date=3 December 2010 | accessdate=21 August 2014 | author=Arun Janardhan}}</ref> He played the lead role in the suspense thriller television show ''2612'' (2012)<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/maninder-singh-play-the-lead-sphere-origins-next-life-ok|title=Maninder Singh to play the lead in Sphere Origins' next for Life OK|date=June 13, 2012|website=Tellychakkar|access-date=September 25, 2017}}</ref> as Randeep Rathore and reprised the same role in the next season titled as ''2613''. In 2014, he was also seen in the popular television show ''[[C.I.D. (Indian TV series)|C.I.D.]]'' <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/gaurav-khanna-and-maninder-singh-enter-cid-senior-cops-duty-582|title=Gaurav Khanna and Maninder Singh to enter CID as senior cops on duty|date=March 24, 2014|website=Tellychakkar|access-date=September 25, 2017}}</ref> portraying senior CID cop.<ref name="CID">{{cite news|url=http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/television/actor-gaurav-khanna-to-enter-cid/|title=Actor Gaurav Khanna & Maninder Singh to enter 'CID' as senior cops|date=30 March 2014|newspaper=[[Indian Express]]|accessdate=21 August 2014}}</ref> He was also seen in Zee TV's ''[[Sanyukt]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/exclusive-meet-the-cast-of-zee-tv-s-newest-nuclear-family-sanyukt-160816|title=Exclusive: Meet the cast of Zee TV's newest 'nuclear family', Sanyukt|date=August 16, 2016|website=Tellychakkar|access-date=September 25, 2017}}</ref> He is currently seen in Star Bharat's ''[[Kya Haal, Mr. Paanchal?]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/shikayat-peti-navratri-drama-mad-rush-kya-haal-mr-panchaal-170915|title=Shikayat Peti, Navratri drama & mad rush in Kya Haal Mr. Panchaal|date=September 15, 2017|website=Tellychakkar|access-date=September 25, 2017}}</ref>
