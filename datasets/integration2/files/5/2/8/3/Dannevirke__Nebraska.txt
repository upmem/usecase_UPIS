'''Dannevirke''' is an [[unincorporated community]] in [[Howard County, Nebraska|Howard County]], [[Nebraska]], United States.<ref>{{GNIS|828577}}</ref>

==History==
A post office was established at Dannevirke in the 1880s.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?task=display&state=NE&county=Howard | title=Howard County | publisher=Jim Forte Postal History | accessdate=13 August 2014}}</ref> A majority of the early settlers being natives of Denmark caused the name of [[Danevirke|Dannevirke]], for the old Danish fortification, to be selected.<ref>{{cite book|last1=Hickey|first1=Donald R.|last2=Wunder|first2=Susan A.|last3=Wunder|first3=John R.|title=Nebraska Moments|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5dtgP41CyK4C&pg=PA118|year=2007|publisher=U of Nebraska Press|isbn=0-8032-1572-X|page=118}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|author=Federal Writers' Project|title=Nebraska: A Guide to the Cornhusker State|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=AZln_ns0-qIC&pg=PA293|year=1939|publisher=WPA|isbn=0-8032-6918-8|page=293}}</ref>
