'''''Apex''''' is the fourth studio album by Canadian [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band [[Unleash the Archers]]. It was released on June 2, 2017, through [[Napalm Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.metaltalk.net/news_seventeen/unleash_the_archers_album_details.php|title=UNLEASH THE ARCHERS UPCOMING ALBUM DETAILS ARE HERE|last=Goldby|first=Steve|website=www.metaltalk.net|access-date=2017-12-29}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://label.napalmrecords.com/blog/?entryID=1003|title=napalmrecords.com :: Newsblog|website=label.napalmrecords.com|language=de|access-date=2017-12-29}}</ref>
