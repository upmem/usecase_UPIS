'''Édouard Le Jeune''' (20 February 1921 – 9 April 2017) was a French [[World War II]] veteran and politician. He was the mayor of [[Dinéault]] from 1953 to 1989, and a senator for [[Finistère]] from 1971 to 1998. He was the founding president of the [[Parc naturel régional d'Armorique]].
[[File:View along Monts d'Arrée 1.jpg|thumb|right|View of the Parc naturel régional d'Armorique, founded by Le Jeune in 1969.]]

==Career==
[[File:Dinéault (29) Mairie.JPG|thumb|right|The new townhall of Dinéault, dedicated by Le Jeune in 1979.]] 
Le Jeune first joined the [[Union of Democrats and Independents – UC]], and later the [[Union for French Democracy]].<ref name="ouestfranceobit"/> He served as the mayor of [[Dinéault]] from 1953 to 1989.<ref name="ouestfranceobit"/><ref name="letelegrammeobit"/> He dedicated a new townhall in 1979.<ref name="letelegrammeobit"/> He established the [[Parc naturel régional d'Armorique]] in 1969,<ref name="letelegrammeobit"/> and he served as its founding president.<ref name="ouestfranceobit"/> He served as a member of the [[Senate (France)|French Senate]], where he represented [[Finistère]] from 1971 to 1998.<ref name="ouestfranceobit"/><ref name="letelegrammeobit"/><ref name="senatebio"/>

Le Jeune received the [[Legion of Honour]] in 1999.<ref name="letelegrammeobit"/>

==Personal life and death==
Le Jeune married a native of Dinéault, Marie-Thérèse, in 1951.<ref name="letelegrammeobit"/> He died on 9 April 2017 in [[Dinéault]].<ref name="ouestfranceobit"/> His funeral was held at the Church in Dinéault on 13 April 2017.<ref name="ouestfranceobit"/>
