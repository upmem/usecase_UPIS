'''Lavry''' ({{lang-ru|Лавры}}; {{lang-et|Laura}}) is a [[types of inhabited localities in Russia|village]] in [[Pechorsky District]] of [[Pskov Oblast]], [[Russia]]. Population: {{ru-census|p2010=1008|p2002=888}}. The [[List of extreme points of Russia|westernmost point of mainland Russia]] is located near the village.  Lavry is located about 400 miles west of the capital city of Moscow.  Lavry is located in the "Europe/Moscow" time zone, which is three hours ahead of standard UTC time (London).
