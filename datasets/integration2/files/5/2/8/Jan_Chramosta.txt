'''Jan Chramosta''' (born 12 October 1990) is a [[Czech republic|Czech]] [[Association football|football]]er, who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|striker]] for [[FK Jablonec|Jablonec]].<ref>[https://fotbal.idnes.cz/chramosta-prestup-jablonec-df3-/fotbal.aspx?c=A180210_082020_fotbal_krb Hotovo. Útočník Chramosta už patří Jablonci]</ref>

In his first season Chramosta scored five goals in nine matches and was eventually voted "Revelation of the Year" at the [[Golden Ball (Czech Republic)|Golden Ball]] awards. He was a member of the [[Czech Republic national under-21 football team|Czech under-21 team]]. He represented the team at the [[2011 UEFA European Under-21 Championship]].<ref>[http://fotbal.idnes.cz/ceskym-fotbalovym-kralem-se-stal-nedved-sesadil-cecha-pdg-/fot_reprez.asp?c=A090730_115131_fot_reprez_min Českým fotbalovým králem se stal Nedvěd, sesadil Čecha]</ref>

In June 2012, Chramosta scored all 5 goals in a 5–1 win over [[Andorra national under-21 football team|Andorra U21]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/under21/season=2013/matches/round=2000192/match=2006843/postmatch/report/index.html#chramosta+steals+show|title=Five-star Chramosta takes Czechs to brink|work=UEFA.com|date=5 June 2012|accessdate=29 August 2012}}</ref>
