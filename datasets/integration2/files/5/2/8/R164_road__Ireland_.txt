The '''R164 road''' is a [[Regional road (Ireland)|regional road]] in [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]], linking [[Kingscourt]] in [[County Cavan]] to the [[N51 road (Ireland)|N51]] near [[Athboy]], [[County Meath]].

==Route==
[[File:IMG R164overBlackwater.jpg|thumb|left|R164 crosses the Blackwater near Kells]]
North to South the route starts in Kingscourt, County Cavan and in 2 km crosses into County Meath. It continues southwards through [[Moynalty]], across the [[Kells Blackwater]] river and into [[Kells, County Meath|Kells]]. 

In Kells it joins the [[R147 road (Ireland)|R147]] at ''Carrick Street'' and continues through ''Castle Street''; turns into the [[N52 road (Ireland)|N52]] at ''Cross Street''; continues through ''Farrell Street'' and ''Bective Street'' before leaving the N52 and heading south along ''Rockfield Street''.

It leaves Kells south through [[Scurlockstown]] and [[Fordstown]] before terminating at the [[N51 road (Ireland)|N51]] 3 km east of [[Athboy]].
