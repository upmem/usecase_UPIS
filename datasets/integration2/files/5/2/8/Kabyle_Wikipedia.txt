The '''Kabyle Wikipedia''' is the [[Kabyle language]] edition of [[Wikipedia]]. Launched in April 2007,<ref>[https://stats.wikimedia.org/EN/TablesWikipediaKAB.htm Statistics Table]</ref> It currently has {{NUMBEROF|ARTICLES|kab|N}} articles, making it the 209th largest [[Wikipedia]] by article count. This Wikipedia has 3 administrators along with 13,671 registered users and 25 active users.<ref>[https://wikistats.wmcloud.org/display.php?t=wp&s=good_desc List of Wikipedias]</ref>
