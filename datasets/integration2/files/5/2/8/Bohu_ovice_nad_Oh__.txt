'''Bohušovice nad Ohří''' ({{lang-de|Bauschowitz an der Eger}}) is a town in the [[Ústí nad Labem Region]] of the [[Czech Republic]]. It has about 2,500 inhabitants. It is located in a flat lowland near left bank of the [[Ohře]] River about 5 km south of [[Litoměřice]].
