'''Łukowo''' {{IPAc-pl|ł|u|'|k|o|w|o}} ({{lang-de|Luckland}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Oborniki]], within [[Oborniki County]], [[Greater Poland Voivodeship]], in west-central Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> 

== Geography ==
It lies approximately {{convert|5|km|mi|0}} east of [[Oborniki]] and {{convert|26|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Poznań]].
