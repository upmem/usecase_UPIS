'''McCoy Park''' is a park in the [[Portsmouth, Portland, Oregon|Portsmouth neighborhood]] of [[Portland, Oregon]], United States. Named for the Oregon Senator [[William McCoy (Oregon politician)|Bill McCoy]] and his wife [[Gladys McCoy|Gladys]],<ref name=PP&R>{{Cite web |url=https://www.portlandoregon.gov/parks/finder/index.cfm?&action=ViewPark&propertyid=1258 |title=McCoy Park |access-date=July 14, 2019 |publisher=[[Portland Parks & Recreation]]}}</ref> the park is located at the center of [[New Columbia (Portland, Oregon)|New Columbia]], a large housing development in Portsmouth.

==Description and history==
The park was acquired in 2005 and features paved paths and picnic tables, a playground, and a basketball court.<ref name=PP&R />

Shootings took place at or near McCoy Park in 2011, 2014, and 2016, two of them fatal.<ref>{{cite news |last=Wilson |first=Kimberly A.
