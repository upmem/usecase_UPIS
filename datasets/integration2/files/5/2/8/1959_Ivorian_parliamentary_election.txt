{{Politics of Côte d'Ivoire}}

'''Parliamentary elections''' were held in [[Ivory Coast]] on 12 April 1959 as a prelude to independence the next year. The [[Democratic Party of Ivory Coast – African Democratic Rally]] was the only party to contest the election, thereby winning all 100 seats. Voter turnout was 94.7%.<ref>[[Dieter Nohlen]], Michael Krennerich & Bernhard Thibaut (1999) ''Elections in Africa: A data handbook'', p308 {{ISBN|0-19-829645-2}}</ref>
