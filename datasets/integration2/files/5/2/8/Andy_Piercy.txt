'''Andy Piercy''' is a British [[singer-songwriter]] who came to prominence in the [[Jesus music|gospel beat]] [[acoustic guitar|acoustic]] [[musical duo|duo]] Ishmael and Andy, together with Ian Smale (Ishmael).

In 1974, Piercy replaced John Leach as guitarist and lead vocalist in [[After the Fire]]. He stayed with that group, later taking on bass duties, until they disbanded in 1983.

Subsequently, Piercy was [[record producer]] for many British Christian musicians, including [[Delirious?]] (their original ''[[Cutting Edge (recordings)|Cutting Edge]]'' recordings and the album ''[[King of Fools (album)|King of Fools]]''), [[Matt Redman]] and Split Level.
