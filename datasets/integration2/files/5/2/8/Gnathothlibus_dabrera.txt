'''''Gnathothlibus dabrera''''' is a [[moth]] of the  family [[Sphingidae]]. It is known from [[Sulawesi]].

The [[wingspan]] is 62–76.8&nbsp;mm for males and 81.5&nbsp;mm for females. It differs from ''[[Gnathothlibus erotus]]'' in its smaller size and darker ground colour. Furthermore, the hindwing upperside has a dark marginal band in the males that is broader than in males of ''Gnathothlibus erotus'', but about as wide as in females of ''Gnathothlibus erotus''.
