'''Karolewo''' {{IPAc-pl|k|a|r|o|'|l|e|w|o}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Rogoźno]], within [[Oborniki County]], [[Greater Poland Voivodeship]], in west-central Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|9|km|mi|0}} north-west of [[Rogoźno]], {{convert|20|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of [[Oborniki]], and {{convert|46|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Poznań]].

The village has a population of 130.
