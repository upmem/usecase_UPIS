'''Gołaszyn''' {{IPAc-pl|g|o|'|ł|a|sz|y|n}} ({{lang-de|Golasdorf}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Oborniki]], within [[Oborniki County]], [[Greater Poland Voivodeship]], in west-central Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|3|km|mi|0}} south-east of [[Oborniki]] and {{convert|27|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Poznań]].
