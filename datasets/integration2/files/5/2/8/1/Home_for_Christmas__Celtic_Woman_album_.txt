'''''Celtic Woman: Home for Christmas''''' is the eighth album released by the group [[Celtic Woman]], released on 9 October 2012.<ref name="CW">[http://www.celticwoman.com/2012/09/holiday-album-to-be-released-october-9th/ Celtic Woman: Home for Christmas on official website] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121010124317/http://www.celticwoman.com/2012/09/holiday-album-to-be-released-october-9th/ |date=10 October 2012 }}</ref> It is the third Christmas album released by the group, but only the second available for international purchase.

''Home for Christmas'' features vocalists [[Chloë Agnew]], [[Lisa Lambe]], [[Méav Ní Mhaolchatha]], and fiddler [[Máiréad Nesbitt]].<ref name="CW"/> This is the first album to feature Ní Mhaolchatha since her departure from the group in 2007 following the release of the DVD/PBS special ''[[Celtic Woman: A Christmas Celebration]]''. It is also the first album not to feature founding member [[Lisa Kelly]], who was at the time on maternity leave. The DVD was released on 29 October 2013 with [[Susan McFadden]], [[Lisa Lambe]], [[Méav Ní Mhaolchatha]], and fiddler [[Máiréad Nesbitt]]. It was released at the same date a special package (CD & DVD)
