'''Akhil Bharat Nepali Ekta Samaj''' (ABNES) is a group founded in 1979 in [[Varanasi]], [[India]], whose stated purpose is to work for the unity amongst, and welfare of [[Nepalese people]] residing in India.<ref name="satp">{{cite web|url=http://www.satp.org/satporgtp/countries/india/terroristoutfits/ABNES.htm |title=Extremist Group&nbsp;— Akhil Bharat Nepali Ekta Samaj (ABNES) |publisher=Satp.org |date= |accessdate=2012-10-09}}</ref> On July 2002 it was declared a [[terrorist organisation]], and banned by the [[Minister of Home Affairs (India)|Ministry of Home Affairs]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mha.gov.in/uniquepage.asp?Id_Pk=292 |title=::Ministry of Home Affairs |publisher=Mha.gov.in |date=2011-12-07 |accessdate=2012-10-09 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130425132802/http://mha.gov.in/uniquepage.asp?id_pk=292 |archivedate=25 April 2013 }}</ref><ref name="satp"/>

Following this designation, Janardhan Thapa, an executive member of the group's central committee, announced that they would change their name and focus on "social and cultural activities".<ref name="satp"/>

Translated into [[English Language|English]] from [[Hindi]], the group is roughly called the 'All India Nepalese Integration Society.'<ref>{{cite web|url=https://translate.google.com/#view=home&op=translate&sl=hi&tl=en&text=अखिल%20भारत%20नेपाली%20एकता%20समा |title = Google Translate}}</ref>
