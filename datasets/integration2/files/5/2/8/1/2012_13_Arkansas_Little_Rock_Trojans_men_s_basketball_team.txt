The '''2012–13 Arkansas–Little Rock Trojans men's basketball team''' represented the [[University of Arkansas at Little Rock]] during the [[2012–13 NCAA Division I men's basketball season]]. The Trojans, led by tenth year head coach [[Steve Shields (basketball)|Steve Shields]], played their home games at the [[Jack Stephens Center]], and were members of the West Division of the [[Sun Belt Conference]]. They finished the season 17–15, 11–9 in Sun Belt play to finish in second place in the West Division. They lost in the quarterfinals of the [[2013 Sun Belt Conference Men's Basketball Tournament|Sun Belt Tournament]] to FIU. Despite the 17 wins and winning record, they did not participate in a post season tournament.
