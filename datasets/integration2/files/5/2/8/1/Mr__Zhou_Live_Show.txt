'''''Mr. Zhou Live Show''''' ({{zh|c=壹周立波秀}}) is a Chinese television live [[talk show]] hosted by stand-up comedian [[Zhou Libo (comedian)|Zhou Libo]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://info.broadcast.hc360.com/2011/12/140915481539.shtml|script-title=zh:《壹周立波秀》热播的原因探析|publisher=|date=December 14, 2011|accessdate=October 9, 2012|language=Chinese}}</ref> It was originally broadcast by [[Phoenix Television]] and [[Dragon Television]] in 2010 and by [[Zhejiang Television]] after 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sd.xinhuanet.com/news/2012-04/15/content_25067225.htm|script-title=zh:周立波担任梦想观察员《壹周立波秀》将调整 |publisher=[[Xinhuanet]]|date=April 15, 2012|accessdate=October 9, 2012|language=Chinese}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.he-nan.com/article_66679.html|script-title=zh:周立波携《壹周立波秀》跳槽至浙江卫视|publisher=河南网|date=April 5, 2012|accessdate=October 9, 2012|language=Chinese}}</ref>

Like his stand-up, Zhou does not have scripts or notes, creating his own material through improvisation. The show also includes guest interviews and Zhou dropped his Shanghai dialect to have a more accessible audience.
