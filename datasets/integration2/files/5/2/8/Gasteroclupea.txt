'''''Gasteroclupea''''' is a genus of prehistoric [[clupeiformes|clupeiform]] [[fish]] that is related to modern [[anchovies]] and [[herring]]s. Its [[fossils]] date back to the [[Maastrichtian]] stage of the Late [[Cretaceous]] period. Fossils of the genus have been found in the [[Yacoraite Formation]] of Argentina and the [[El Molino Formation]] of Bolivia.<ref name=FWGasteroclupea>[http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=35450 ''Gasteroclupea''] at [[Fossilworks]].org</ref>
