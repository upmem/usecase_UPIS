'''Christian Valdemar Svendsen''' (13 July 1890 – 28 June 1959) was a [[Denmark|Danish]] [[gymnast]] who competed in the [[1912 Summer Olympics]].

He was part of the Danish team, which won the bronze medal in the gymnastics men's team, free system event.
