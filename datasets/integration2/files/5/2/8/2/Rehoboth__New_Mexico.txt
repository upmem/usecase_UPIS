'''Rehoboth''' is an [[Unincorporated area|unincorporated community]] in [[McKinley County, New Mexico|McKinley County]], [[New Mexico]], United States.<ref name="gnis"/> Rehoboth is located along [[Interstate 40]] {{convert|5|mi|km}} east of downtown [[Gallup, New Mexico|Gallup]]. Rehoboth has a [[post office]] with [[ZIP code]] 87322.<ref name="GR7">{{cite web|url=http://tools.usps.com/go/ZipLookupAction!input.action|title=USPS - Look Up a ZIP Code|accessdate=2012-02-15|author=United States Postal Service|year=2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Postmaster Finder - Post Offices by ZIP Code|url=http://webpmt.usps.gov/pmt008.cfm|publisher=[[United States Postal Service]]|accessdate=August 24, 2013}}</ref> The [[Rehoboth Christian School]] is located in Rehoboth. it has a population of 56 permanent residents.
