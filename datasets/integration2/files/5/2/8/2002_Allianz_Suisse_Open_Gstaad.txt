The '''2002 Allianz Suisse Open Gstaad''' was a men's [[tennis]] tournament played on [[Clay court|outdoor clay courts]] at the [[Roy Emerson Arena]] in [[Gstaad]] in [[Switzerland]] and was part of the [[ATP International Series|International Series]] of the [[2002 ATP Tour]]. It was the 57th edition of the tournament and was held from 8 July until 14 July 2002. [[Àlex Corretja]] won the singles title.

==Finals==
===Singles===
{{main|2002 Allianz Suisse Open Gstaad – Singles}}
{{flagicon|ESP}} [[Àlex Corretja]] defeated {{flagicon|ARG}} [[Gastón Gaudio]] 6–3, 7–6<sup>(7–3)</sup>, 7–6<sup>(7–3)</sup>
* It was Corretja's 1st title of the year and the 19th of his career.

===Doubles===
{{main|2002 Allianz Suisse Open Gstaad – Doubles}}
{{flagicon|AUS}} [[Joshua Eagle]] / {{flagicon|CZE}} [[David Rikl]] defeated {{flagicon|ITA}} [[Massimo Bertolini]] / {{flagicon|ITA}} [[Cristian Brandi]] 7–6<sup>(7–5)</sup>, 6–4
* It was Eagle's 1st title of the year and the 3rd of his career. It was Rikl's 3rd title of the year and the 25th of his career.
