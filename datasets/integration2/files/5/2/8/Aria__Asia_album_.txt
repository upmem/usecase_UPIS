'''''Aria''''' is the fifth studio album by British rock band [[Asia (band)|Asia]], released in April 1994 in the United Kingdom by Bullet Proof Records and in 1995 in the United States by Mayhem Recordings. Unlike its [[Aqua (Asia album)|predecessor]], which was recorded with the help of several guest musicians, ''Aria'' features a stable line-up consisting of vocalist and bassist [[John Payne (singer)|John Payne]], keyboard player [[Geoff Downes]], guitarist [[Al Pitrelli]] and drummer [[Mike Sturgis|Michael Sturgis]].
