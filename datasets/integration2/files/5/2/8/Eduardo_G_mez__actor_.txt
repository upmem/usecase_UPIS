'''Eduardo Gómez Manzano''' (27 July 1951 – 28 July 2019) was a Spanish [[actor]] who was born in [[Madrid]], [[Spain]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Eduardo Gómez|url=https://www.formulatv.com/personas/eduardo-gomez/|access-date=2020-11-02|website=FormulaTV|language=es}}</ref>

==Early life and career==
Gómez began acting late in life. In his early forties, he was accompanying a friend to a shooting. The production team persuaded him to say a few phrases to the camera. [[Andrés Pajares]], a comedian and actor, saw him during the production and asked him whether he acted. Gómez answered that he was not an actor but if he paid him, he would act. Of the experience, Gómez has said, "The next day I went, he paid me and to this day! [...] What has happened to me doesn't happen to many people. I'm very happy."<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.diezminutos.wanadoo.es/reportajes/2547_1.html |title=Eduardo Gómez 'Si fuese guapo tendría que quitarme a las mujeres de encima' |access-date=4 February 2006 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20050117024901/http://www.diezminutos.wanadoo.es/reportajes/2547_1.html |archive-date=17 January 2005 |date=13 January 2005 |issue=2787 |language=es |magazine=[[Diez Minutos]] |publisher=[[Hachette Filipacchi]] |agency=[[Wanadoo]]}}</ref>

He went on to perform in small parts in Spanish television and cinema, including parts in the [[Goya]] Award-winning movies ''[[La comunidad (film)|La comunidad]]'' and ''[[Butterfly (1999 film)|La lengua de las mariposas]]''. He later appeared in a sequel of [[Santiago Segura]]'s ''Torrente'' series, ''[[Torrente 3: El Protector]]''. He also had a part in the movie version of the legendary Spanish [[comic strip]] ''[[Mortadelo y Filemón]]''.

He was cast in the part of Emilio's father in the hit Spanish comedy ''[[Aquí no hay quien viva]]''.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.lasexta.com/noticias/cultura/mejores-momentazos-eduardo-gomez-aqui-hay-quien-viva_201907285d3d703d0cf2ba8e051618a3.html |title=Los mejores momentazos de Eduardo Gómez en 'Aquí no hay quien viva' |location=Madrid |date=28 July 2019 |access-date=2 August 2019 |newspaper=[[La Sexta]] Noticias |language=es |publisher=[[Atresmedia Corporación de Medios de Comunicación]], S.A}}</ref> Originally a small, recurring role, Gómez joined the regular cast after the first series and his character of Mariano is now widely recognized in Spain for which he won Best Actor on Unión de Actores y Actrices.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/elmundo/2005/06/21/cultura/1119312678.html |title='Aquí no hay quien viva', 'Mar adentro' y 'Yo Claudio' vencen en los Premios de la Unión de Actores |author=Europa Press |author-link=Europa Press (news agency) |date=21 June 2005 |access-date=2 August 2019 |newspaper=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]] |location=Madrid |language=es |publisher=Mundinteractivos, S.A.}}</ref> He then played Maxi in ''[[La que se avecina]]'' from 2007 to 2013.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.hoycinema.com/actualidad/noticias/eduardo-gomez-maxi-abandona-que-20130813-701039.html |title=Eduardo Gómez (Maxi) abandona 'La que se avecina' |date=16 August 2013 |access-date=28 July 2019 |author=Europa Press |author-link=Europa Press (news agency) |language=es |work=Hoy Cinema |publisher=[[ABC (Spain)|ABC]] |agency=[[Vocento]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130916012544/http://www.hoycinema.com/actualidad/noticias/eduardo-gomez-maxi-abandona-que-20130813-701039.html |archive-date=16 September 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

In 2013 he parodied [[Capitán Alatriste]] in ''[[Spanish Movie]]'' along [[Alexandra Jiménez]], [[Carlos Areces]], [[Joaquín Reyes (actor)|Joaquín Reyes]] and [[Michelle Jenner]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/elmundo/2009/11/23/videos/1258990120.html |title=De 'Muchachada Nui' a 'Spanish movie' |date=23 November 2009 |access-date=2 August 2019 |newspaper=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]] |language=es |publisher=[[Unidad Editorial Información General]], S.L.U.}}</ref> In February 2015 he was cast as Juanito in the [[Cuatro (TV channel)|Cuatro]] TV program ''Gym Tony'' along Carmen Ruiz and [[Pepa Rus]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Eduardo Gómez, Carmen Ruiz y Pepa Rus se apuntan al 'Gym Tony' |url=http://ecoteuve.eleconomista.es/series/noticias/6468230/02/15/Eduardo-Gomez-Carmen-Ruiz-y-Pepa-Rus-se-apuntan-al-Gym-Tony.html#.Kku8iHfYkt667Kr |newspaper=[[El Economista (Spain)|El Economista]] |date=11 February 2015 |access-date=6 April 2015 |publisher=Editorial Ecoprensa, S.A. |agency=Ecoteuve}}</ref>

He appeared posthumously in the TV programme ''Trabajo temporal''.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://vertele.eldiario.es/noticias/trabajo-postumo-Eduardo-Gomez-entrevista_0_2144185572.html |title=El trabajo póstumo de Eduardo Gómez en TV, este lunes en La 1 que lo programó con anterioridad |date=28 July 2019 |access-date=2 August 2019 |newspaper=[[El Diario (Spain)|El Diario]] |publisher=Vertele! |language=es}}</ref>

==Private life and death==
Gómez had one son, Héctor, from a previous marriage.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.semana.es/corazon/el-hijo-de-eduardo-gomez-cancer-gladiador-20190728-002119443/ |title=El hijo de Eduardo Gómez desvela que su padre luchó contra el cáncer "como un gladiador" |first=Laura |last=Ortiz |date=28 July 2019 |access-date=29 July 2019 |magazine=[[Semana]] |language=es |publisher=RBA Entertainment}}</ref> He was an [[Atlético de Madrid]] fan.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://as.com/futbol/2014/02/07/primera/1391738733_137927.html |title=Eduardo Gómez: "Cristiano Ronaldo alucinó al verme" |first=A. |last=Mérida |first2=G. |last2=Pose |date=7 February 2014 |access-date=2 August 2019 |newspaper=[[AS (newspaper)|AS]] |language=es}}</ref>

He died on 28 July 2019 from [[laryngeal cancer]],<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.lavanguardia.com/television/20190730/463773543512/eduardo-gomez-muerte-fallecimiento-aqui-no-hay-quien-viva-la-que-se-avecina-trabajo-temporal-tve.html |title=Así fue la aparición póstuma de Eduardo Gómez en televisión |first=Nacho |last=Molina |location=Madrid |date=30 July 2019 |access-date=2 August 2019 |newspaper=[[La Vanguardia]] |language=es}}</ref> one day after his 68th birthday.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.lavanguardia.com/gente/20190728/463741958158/hijo-eduardo-gomez-lucha-cancer-gladiador.html |title=El hijo de Eduardo Gómez dice que luchó contra el cáncer "como un gladiador" |date=28 July 2019 |access-date=28 July 2019 |newspaper=[[La Vanguardia]] |language=es |author=EFE |author-link=EFE}}</ref> The funeral was assisted by his friends [[Alberto Caballero|Alberto]], [[Laura Caballero]], [[Nacho Guerreros]], [[Cristina Castaño]], [[Vanesa Romero]], [[Nathalie Seseña]], [[Malena Alterio]], Laura Pamplona, [[Melani Olivares]], and [[Fernando Ramallo]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.diezminutos.es/famosos-corazon/famosos-espanoles/g28529914/eduardo-gomez-reune-familiares-amigos-capilla-ardiente/ |title=Eduardo Gómez reúne a familiares y amigos en su capilla ardiente |date=28 July 2019 |access-date=29 July 2019 |magazine=[[Diez Minutos]] |language=es |publisher=[[Hearst Magazines International]]}}</ref> He was cremated in [[Cementerio de la Almudena|La Almudena]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.telecinco.es/informativos/sociedad/eduardo-gomez-incinerado-cementerio-almudena_18_2794320017.html |title=Eduardo Gómez será incinerado este lunes en el cementerio de La Almudena |date=29 July 2019 |access-date=29 July 2019 |work=[[Telecinco]] |publisher=[[Mediaset]] |language=es}}</ref>
