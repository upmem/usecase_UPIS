'''Siu Lun''' ({{zh|t=兆麟}}) is an [[MTR]] [[Light Rail (MTR)|Light Rail]] stop located at ground level at Tuen Mun Heung Sze Wui Road between Siu Lun Court and Tsui Ning Garden in [[Tuen Mun District]]. It began service on 17 November 1991 and belongs to Zone 1.
