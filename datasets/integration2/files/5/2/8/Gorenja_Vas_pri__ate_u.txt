'''Gorenja Vas pri Čatežu''' ({{IPA-sl|ɡɔˈɾeːnja ˈʋaːs pɾi ˈtʃaːtɛʒu|pron}}; {{lang-sl|Gorenja vas pri Čatežu}}) is a small settlement to the east of [[Čatež, Trebnje|Čatež]] in the [[Municipality of Trebnje]] in eastern [[Slovenia]]. Traditionally the area was part [[Lower Carniola]] and is now included in the [[Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region]].<ref>[http://www.trebnje.si/ Trebnje municipal site]</ref>

==Name==
Gorenja Vas pri Čatežu was attested in historical sources as ''Obern Szates'' in 1420, ''Ober Zathes'' in 1425, ''Ober Tschattes'' in 1448, and ''Obern Tschates'' in 1465.<ref>{{cite web |title=Gorenja Vas pri Čatežu |url=https://topografija.zrc-sazu.si/ |website=Slovenska historična topografija |publisher=ZRC SAZU Zgodovinski inštitut Milka Kosa |accessdate=November 29, 2020}}</ref> The name of the settlement was changed from ''Gorenja  vas'' to ''Gorenja vas pri Čatežu'' in 1953.<ref>''Spremembe naselij 1948–95''. 1996. Database. Ljubljana: Geografski inštitut ZRC SAZU, DZS.</ref>
