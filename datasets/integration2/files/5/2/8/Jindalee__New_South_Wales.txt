'''Jindalee''' is a locality in the [[Riverina]] district of [[New South Wales]], [[Australia]]. The [[Main Southern railway line, New South Wales|Main South railway line]] passes through the area, and a passenger station was located there between 1896 and 1970.<ref>[http://www.nswrail.net/locations/show.php?name=NSW:Jindalee&line=NSW:main_south:0 Jindalee station]. NSWrail.net, accessed 29 August 2009.</ref> The town's name is derived from an aboriginal word meaning 'bare hill'.<ref>{{NSW GNR|id=MaYboesEJP|title=Jamberoo|accessdate=11 October 2009}}</ref>
