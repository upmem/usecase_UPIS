'''Columbia Park''' is a park located in north [[Portland, Oregon]], United States.

==History==
The city of [[Albina, Oregon|Albina]] purchased the land in 1891, prior to its annexation by the city of Portland. In 1909 G.H. Hoch, head gardener for [[Washington Park (Portland, Oregon)|Washington Park]], oversaw the design, which he "patterned ... after a famous [[List of parks and gardens in Berlin|park in Berlin]]."<ref name=PPR>{{Cite web |url= http://www.portlandonline.com/parks/finder/index.cfm?action=ViewPark&PropertyID=17 |title= Columbia Park|accessdate=December 9, 2011|publisher=[[Portland Parks & Recreation]]}}</ref>
