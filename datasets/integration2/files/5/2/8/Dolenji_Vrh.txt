'''Dolenji Vrh''' ({{IPA-sl|dɔˈleːnji ˈʋəɾx|pron}}) is a small settlement in the hills south of [[Trebnje]] in eastern [[Slovenia]]. The area is part of the historical [[Lower Carniola]] region. The entire [[Municipality of Trebnje]] is now included in the [[Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region]].<ref>[http://www.trebnje.si/ Trebnje municipal site]</ref>
