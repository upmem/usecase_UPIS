'''Dilbar Abdurahmonova''' (1 May 1936 – 20 March 2018) was a Russian-born Uzbekistani violinist and conductor.

Born in [[Moscow]] to parents Gulyam Abdurahmanov and Zuhra Faiziyeva, she began learning the violin at the age of 10, and was a student of [[Mukhtar Ashrafi]]. Abdurahmonova attended the [[State Conservatory of Uzbekistan]] and performed frequently at the [[Navoi Theatre]].<ref name="nuzuz">{{cite news|title=Ташкент прощается с первой на Востоке женщиной-дирижёром|url=https://nuz.uz/kultura-i-iskusstvo/31245-tashkent-proschaetsya-s-pervoy-na-vostoke-zhenschinoy-dirizherom.html|accessdate=20 March 2018|publisher=nuz.uz|date=20 March 2018|language=ru}}</ref> She was designated a [[People's Artist of the USSR]] in 1977.

Abdurahmonova last performed in December 2017, and was sent to hospital in February 2018. She died on 20 March 2018, aged 81.<ref name="nuzuz"/>
