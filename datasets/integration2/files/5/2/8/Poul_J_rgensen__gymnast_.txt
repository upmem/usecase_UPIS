'''Poul Preben Jørgensen''' (17 February 1892 – 6 October 1973) was a [[Denmark|Danish]] [[gymnast]] who competed in the [[1912 Summer Olympics]].

He was part of the Danish team, which won the bronze medal in the gymnastics men's team, free system event.
