'''Giuseppina''' is an [[Italian language|Italian]] feminine given name. It is the Italian version of the [[English language|English]] name [[Josephine (given name)|Josephine]].

==Variations==
*Feminine: Giuseppa, Geppa, Geppina, Beppa, Beppina, Peppa, Peppina, Pina, Pinella, Pinetta, Pinuccia, Giusi, Giusy.
*Masculine: Giuseppe.
