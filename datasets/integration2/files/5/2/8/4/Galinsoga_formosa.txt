'''''Galinsoga formosa''''' is a rare Mexican species of flowering plant in the [[Asteraceae|daisy family]]. It has been found only the State of [[Oaxaca]] in southwestern [[Mexico]].<ref name=c>[https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/663378#page/342/mode/1up Canne-Hilliker, Judith Marie. 1977. Rhodora 79(819): 338-340] in English, distribution map on page 339</ref><ref>Jørgensen, P. M., M. H. Nee & S. G. Beck. (eds.) 2014. Catálogo de las plantas vasculares de Bolivia, Monographs in systematic botany from the Missouri Botanical Garden 127(1–2): i–viii, 1–1744</ref>

==Description==
''Galinsoga formosa'' is a branching annual herb up to {{convert|100|cm|in|abbr=on}} tall. Leaves are up to {{convert|11|cm|in|abbr=on}} long. Flower heads are up to {{convert|22|mm|in|abbr=on}} across. Each head has 5-15 white (sometimes with a purplish underside) ray flowers surrounding up to 100 yellow disc flowers.<ref name=c/>
