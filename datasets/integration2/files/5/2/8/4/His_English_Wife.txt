'''''His English Wife''''' (German: '''''Die Lady ohne Schleier''''', Swedish: '''''Hans engelska fru''''') is a 1927 German-Swedish [[silent film|silent]] [[drama film]] directed by [[Gustaf Molander]] and starring [[Lil Dagover]], [[Gösta Ekman]] and [[Karin Swanström]].<ref>Donoghue p.viii</ref>

The film's sets were designed by the [[art director]] [[Vilhelm Bryde]].
