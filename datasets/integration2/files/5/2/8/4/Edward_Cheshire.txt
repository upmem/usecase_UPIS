'''Alderman Edward Cheshire, J.P.''' was a nineteenth century British [[brewer]] who was Mayor of [[Smethwick]], England, from 1902 to 1903.<ref>Sandwell History and Archive Service; description on 1902 photograph on [http://blackcountryhistory.org/collections/getrecord/GB146_PHS_10073/] (Accessed 16/07/2015)</ref>  

Cheshire is commemorated in the street name Cheshire Road which runs between Smethwick Council House and [[Smethwick Old Church]].  His local benefactions include an extension to the Churchyard of Smethwick Old Church,<ref>Bryan Jones; ''A Brief History of Smethwick Old Church'',; page 5.</ref> and the Edward Cheshire Nurses Home, dated 1903.
