'''Nienawiszcz''' {{IPAc-pl|ń|e1|'|n|a|w|i|sz|cz}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Rogoźno]], within [[Oborniki County]], [[Greater Poland Voivodeship]], in west-central Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) &ndash; TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|8|km|mi|0}} south of [[Rogoźno]], {{convert|13|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} east of [[Oborniki]], and {{convert|32|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Poznań]].
