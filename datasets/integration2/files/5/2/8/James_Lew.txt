'''James Jene Fae Lew'''<ref>[http://www.filmreference.com/film/2/James-Lew.html James Lew Biography (1952-)<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> (born September 6, 1952) is an American martial arts actor. He has made 80 on-screen film and television appearances and 46 more as a stunt coordinator or stunt double. He has done choreography for movies like ''[[Get Smart (film)|Get Smart]]'', ''[[Killers (2010 film)|Killers]]'' and the cult classic ''[[Big Trouble In Little China]]'', as well as television shows such as ''National Geographic's Fight Science'', ''[[The Crow: Stairway to Heaven]]'', and ''[[Entourage (U.S. TV series)|Entourage]]''. He does [[Shaolin Kung-Fu]], [[Tae Kwon Do]], [[Jeet Kune Do]], [[Hapkido]], and [[Boxing]]. He also trained [[Brad Pitt]] with sword fighting for the movie ''[[Troy (film)|Troy]]''.
