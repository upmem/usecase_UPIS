'''Bělá pod Bezdězem''' ({{lang-de|Weisswasser}}) is a town in the [[Central Bohemian Region]] of the [[Czech Republic]]. It has about 4,800 inhabitants. The centre of the town is historically significant and is protected by law as Urban monument zone.

==Etymology==
The town's name is derived from the river of local significance Bělá and the nearby hill Velký Bezděz.

==Notable people==
*[[Václav Trégl]] (1902–1979), actor
*[[Vladimír Pachman]] (1918–1984), chess publicist
*[[Luděk Pachman]] (1924–2003), Czech-German chess grandmaster
