The '''SFCA Lignel 31''' was a [[France|French]], single engine, [[monoplane#Wing position|low wing monoplane]], one of a series of this type built by [[Société Française de Construction Aéronautique|SFCA]] in the 1930s. It was  capable of [[aerobatics]] but was primarily a [[air racing|racing aircraft]].
