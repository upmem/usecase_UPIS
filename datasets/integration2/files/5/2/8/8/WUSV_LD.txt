'''WUSV-LD''', [[ultra high frequency|UHF]] [[Digital terrestrial television|digital]] channel 16, is a [[Low-power broadcasting#Television|low-powered]] [[Antenna TV]]-[[network affiliate|affiliated]] [[television station]] [[City of license|licensed]] to [[Clarksburg, West Virginia|Clarksburg]], [[West Virginia]], United States.<ref>[http://antennatv.tv/interactive-affiliate-map/ Interactive Affiliate Map]. [[Antenna TV]]. Retrieved September 9, 2019.</ref> The station is owned by TTV, [[Limited liability company|LLC]]. WUSV-LD's transmitter is located in East View, West Virginia, on a tower shared with [[WVUX-LD]] and [[Cornerstone Television]] [[owned-and-operated station]] [[W46EJ-D]].

==History==
The station's [[construction permit]] was issued on September 22, 2011 under the callsign '''W16DH-D'''. The station's callsign was changed to the current '''WUSV-LD''' on December 28 of that year.<ref name=wusv>[https://www.rabbitears.info/market.php?request=station_search&callsign=187816 Digital TV Market Listing for WUSV-LD]</ref>

The station signed on at some time in the mid-2010s as an Antenna TV affiliate.
