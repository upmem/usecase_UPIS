'''''Nodocolaspis''''' is a [[genus]] of [[leaf beetle]]s in the subfamily [[Eumolpinae]]. They are known from [[Central America]] and [[South America]].<ref name="Bechyné1953">{{cite journal|last=Bechyné|first=J.|year=1953|title=Katalog der neotropischen Eumolpiden (Col. Phytoph. Chrysomeloidea)|url=https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/49645311|journal=Entomologische Arbeiten aus dem Museum G. Frey|volume=4|pages=26–303|language=German}}</ref> The genus was first established by the Czech entomologist [[Jan Bechyné]] in 1949, as a relative of ''Nodonota'' (now known as ''[[Brachypnoea]]'') containing species formerly placed in ''[[Colaspis]]''.<ref name="Bechyné1949"/>

The type species, ''[[Nodocolaspis costipennis|N. costipennis]]'', was originally described as "''Colaspis costipennis''" by Lefèvre in 1877, which makes the name a junior homonym of ''[[Colaspis costipennis]]'' [[George Robert Crotch|Crotch]], 1873; however, Jan Bechyné considered it worthless to correct the homonymy after having established a new genus for the Lefèvre species.<ref name="Bechyné1951">{{cite journal|first=J.|last=Bechyné|year=1951|title=Liste provisoire des Eumolpides de Bolivie et observations diverses sur les espèces de l'Amérique du Sud (Col. Phytophaga)|url=https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/45935465|journal=Entomologische Arbeiten aus dem Museum G. Frey Tutzing bei München|volume=2|pages=227–352}}</ref> Separately, [[Doris Holmes Blake]] renamed the Lefèvre species to "''Colaspis lophodes''" in 1974.<ref name="Blake1974">{{cite journal|last=Blake|first=D. H.|year=1974|title=The costate species of ''Colaspis'' in the United States (Coleoptera: Chrysomelidae)|url=https://repository.si.edu/bitstream/handle/10088/5252/SCtZ-0181-Lo_res.pdf?sequence=2&isAllowed=y|journal=[[Smithsonian Contributions to Zoology]]|issue=181|pages=1–24|doi=10.5479/si.00810282.181}}</ref>

The generic placement of the [[Central America]]n species ''[[Nodocolaspis femoralis|N. femoralis]]'' and ''[[Nodocolaspis impressa|N. impressa]]'' is uncertain; they were transferred to ''Nodocolaspis'' from ''Colaspis'' by Jan Bechyné in 1953 and 1958, but Flowers (1996) disagreed with the validity of the transfers and included them in ''Colaspis'', where they have been traditionally placed.<ref name="Flowers1996">{{cite journal|last=Flowers|first=R.W.|year=1996|title=The subfamily Eumolpinae (Coleoptera: Chrysomelidae) in Central America|journal=Revista de Biología Tropical Special Publication|url=http://www.sidalc.net/cgi-bin/wxis.exe/?IsisScript=oet.xis&method=post&formato=2&cantidad=1&expresion=mfn=009919|volume=2|pages=1–59|access-date=December 31, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|first1=Martijn|last1=Van Roie|first2=Frederik|last2=De Wint|first3=Ayse|last3=Güngor|first4=Charlotte|last4=Huyghe|first5=Wouter|last5=Dekoninck|first6=Lukáš|last6=Sekerka|year=2019|title=An annotated checklist of the leaf beetles (Coleoptera, Chrysomelidae) from El Salvador, with additions from the Bechyné collection in the Royal Belgian Institute of Natural Sciences|journal=[[ZooKeys]]|issue=856|pages=137–196|doi=10.3897/zookeys.856.32017|pmc=6591217|pmid=31258371}}</ref>
