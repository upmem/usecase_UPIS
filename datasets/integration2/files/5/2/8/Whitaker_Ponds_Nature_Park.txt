'''Whitaker Ponds Nature Park''' is a park located in northeast [[Portland, Oregon|Portland]], [[Oregon]]. The park is maintained by [[Portland Parks & Recreation]] and volunteers from the Columbia Slough Watershed Council.<ref name=PPR>{{Cite web|url=http://www.portlandonline.com/parks/finder/index.cfm?action=ViewPark&PropertyID=856|title=Whitaker Ponds Nature Park|accessdate=December 9, 2011|publisher=[[Portland Parks & Recreation]]}}</ref>
