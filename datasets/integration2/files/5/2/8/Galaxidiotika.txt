'''Galaxidiotika''' ({{lang-el|Γαλαξιδιώτικα}}) was an old [[List of neighbourhoods in Patras|neighbourhood]] in the city of [[Patras]]. Many residents came from [[Galaxidi]], a town in [[Phocis]] in [[Central Greece]], hence the origin of its name.
