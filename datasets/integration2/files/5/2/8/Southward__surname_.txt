'''[[Southward]]''' is an adjective meaning "movement towards the south".

People with the name '''Southward''' include:
*[[Ferguson Southward]], English rugby league footballer of the 1920s
*[[Ike Southward]], English rugby league footballer of the 1950s and 1960s
*[[John Southward]], English writer
*[[Kirsha Southward]], British actress
*[[Walter Southward]], English Archdeacon in New Zealand
