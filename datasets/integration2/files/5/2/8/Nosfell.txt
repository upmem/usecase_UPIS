'''Nosfell''' (born 1 December 1977 in [[Saint-Ouen, Seine-Saint-Denis|Saint-Ouen]], [[France]]) is a French [[Rock music|rock]] musician who performs in a group with Pierre Lebourgeois (cellist) and Orkhan Murat (drummer). His full stage name, ''Labyala Fela Da Jawid Fel'', means "The one who walks and heals".<ref>{{in lang|fr}} [http://www.rocknfrance.com/critique-album-nosfell-pomaie-klokochazia-balek-chronique-f10180.php Nosfell] on rocknfrance.com.</ref>

== Career ==
Born in [[Île-de-France (region)|the Parisian suburbia]], Nosfell studied Asian languages<ref>{{in lang|fr}} http://www.makingoz.com/le_nectar_du_chaos/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=33&Itemid=39&limit=1&limitstart=0</ref> but became famous as a [[cabaret]] performer and musician. He played major venues including the [[Paris Olympia]], opening for [[Tryo]], and the [[Parc des Princes]], opening for the [[Red Hot Chili Peppers]] and [[Pixies (band)|Pixies]]. At the [[Printemps de Bourges]] music festival he was awarded the "Attention talent scène" prize. His first self-promoted LP, ''Pomaïe Klokochazia balek'', was released in 2004, and he then signed to record label [[V2 Records]]. He released two further albums, ''Kälin Bla Lemsnit Dünfel Labyanit'' (2006) and ''Nosfell'' (2009).

== Music ==
Nosfell's musical influences include [[blues]], [[Folk music|folk]], [[funk]], [[African music]], [[Scat singing|scat]], [[human beatbox]], and other traditions.<ref name="makingoz.com">{{in lang|fr}} http://www.makingoz.com/le_nectar_du_chaos/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=33&Itemid=39&limit=1&limitstart=1</ref> His work is characterised by his use of a distinctive invented language, "Klokobetz", which may be based on Japanese and German.<ref name="makingoz.com"/> He also sings in English and French.

His third album, Nosfell (produced by [[Alain Johannes]]), displays clearer rock influences than his other works. [[Josh Homme]], [[Brody Dalle]] and [[Daniel Darc]] guest star on two of the songs. The album's release was accompanied by the publication of a book, ''Le Lac aux Velies'', which Nosfell wrote with Ludovic Debeurme to elaborate the stories he narrates on stage.
