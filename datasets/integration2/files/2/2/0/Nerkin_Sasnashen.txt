'''Nerkin Sasnashen''' ({{lang-hy|Ներքին Սասնաշեն}}); formerly known as ''Nerkin Karakoymaz'' and ''Nerkin Sasunashen'', is a village in the [[Aragatsotn Province]] of [[Armenia]]. It is home to the ruins of a 7th-century Armenian monastery. The village contains a granite memorial marking the crash site of a [[1958 C-130 shootdown incident|United States Air Force C-130 shot down by Soviet MiG-17s on 2 September 1958]] with the loss of 17 U.S. personnel.
