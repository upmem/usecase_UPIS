'''Dynamic Shooting Sport Federation of Hungary''', [[Hungarian language|Hungarian]] '''Magyar Dinamikus Lövészsport Szövetség''', is the [[Hungary|Hungarian]] association for [[practical shooting]] under the [[International Practical Shooting Confederation]] (IPSC)<ref>[http://www.ipsc.org/ipsc/regions.php IPSC.org :: Regions]</ref> as well as [[metallic silhouette shooting]] under the [[International Metallic Silhouette Shooting Union]] (IMSSU).
