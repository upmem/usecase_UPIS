'''Nandasmo Futbol Clube''' is a [[Nicaragua]]n [[association football|football]] team currently playing in the [[Nicaraguan Primera División]]. They are based in [[Nandasmo]].

==History==
Nandasmo was promoted to the Primera Division after the 2015–2016 season. [http://www.laprensa.com.ni/2016/07/06/deportes/2063626-asi-nandasmo-fc-nuevo-equipo-la-primera-division]  However, they were immediately relegated after finishing dead last in both the Apertura and Clausura.
