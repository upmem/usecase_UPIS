'''Wang Shiying''' ({{zh|王世英}}) (November 13, 1905 – March 26, 1968) was a [[People's Republic of China]] politician. He was born in [[Hongtong County]], [[Linfen]], [[Shanxi]]. He was a graduate of the [[Whampoa Military Academy]]. He participated in the [[Northern Expedition]], [[Second Sino-Japanese War]] and [[Chinese Civil War]]. He was a delegate to the [[1st National People's Congress]] and [[2nd National People's Congress]]. He was governor of his home province.

He was interrogated by radical elements during the Cultural Revolution and died during the struggle in 1968.
