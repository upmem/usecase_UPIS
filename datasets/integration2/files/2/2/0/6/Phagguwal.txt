'''Phagguwal''' is a village located in the [[Villages in Ludhiana West Tehsil|Ludhiana West tehsil]], of [[Ludhiana district]], [[Punjab India|Punjab]].<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.censusindia.gov.in/pca/SearchDetails.aspx?Id=37372 | title=Phagguwal | work=censusindia.gov.in | accessdate=25 July 2016}}</ref>

==Administration==
The village is [[Administration (government)|administrated]] by a [[Sarpanch]] who is an [[elected representative]] of village as per [[constitution of India]] and [[Panchayati raj (India)]].
