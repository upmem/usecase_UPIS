'''''Diplodonta semiaspera''''', or the pimpled diplodon, is a species of [[bivalve]] [[mollusc]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Ungulinidae]]. It can be found in Caribbean waters, ranging from southern [[Florida]] to the [[West Indies]].<ref>Abbott, R.T. & Morris, P.A. ''A Field Guide to Shells: Atlantic and Gulf Coasts and the West Indies.'' New York: Houghton Mifflin, 1995. 46.</ref>
