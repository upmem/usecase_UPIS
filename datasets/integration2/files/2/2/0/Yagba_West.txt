'''Yagba West''' is a [[Local Government Areas of Nigeria|Local Government Area]] in [[Kogi State]], [[Nigeria]], in the west of the state adjoining [[Kwara State]]. Its headquarters is in the town of Odo Ere.

It has an area of 1,276&nbsp;km{{sup|2}} and a population of 149,023 at the 2006 census.<ref>[http://www.statoids.com/yng.html HASC, population, area and Headquarters Statoids]</ref>  By 2016, the population grew to 188,900.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.citypopulation.de/php/nigeria-admin.php?adm1id=NGA023|title=Kogi (State, Nigeria) - Population Statistics, Charts, Map and Location|website=www.citypopulation.de|language=en|access-date=2018-09-18}}</ref>

The [[postal code]] of the area is 262.<ref>{{cite web | title = Post Offices- with map of LGA | work = | publisher = NIPOST | url = http://www.nipost.gov.ng/PostCode.aspx | accessdate = 2009-10-20 }}</ref>
