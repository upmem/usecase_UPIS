During the '''[[1976 Summer Olympics]]''' in [[Montreal|Montreal, Quebec]], Canada, '''[[Morocco]]''', along with many other countries, boycotted due to the participation of [[New Zealand at the 1976 Summer Olympics|New Zealand]], which still had sporting links with [[South Africa]].

Athletes from [[Cameroon]], [[Egypt]], Morocco, and [[Tunisia]] competed on July 18–20 before these nations withdrew from the Games.
