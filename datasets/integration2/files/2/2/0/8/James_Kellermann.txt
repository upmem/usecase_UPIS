'''James Kellermann''' (born 11 December 1995) is an English professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Kidderminster Harriers F.C.|Kidderminster Harriers]] as a [[midfielder]].

==Career==
Born in [[Hendon]],<ref name = "SW">{{Soccerway|james-kellerman/334050|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref> Kellermann spent his early career with Kidderminster Lions and [[Stourport Swifts F.C.|Stourport Swifts]] before signing for [[Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C.|Wolverhampton Wanderers]] at the age of 14.<ref name = "Aldershot">{{cite web|url=https://www.theshots.co.uk/jim-kellermann/|title=SHOTS SIGN WOLVES' KELLERMANN|date=5 July 2016|accessdate=24 May 2018|publisher=Aldershot Town F.C.}}</ref> Following his release from Wolves he signed for [[Aldershot Town F.C.|Aldershot Town]].<ref name = "Aldershot"/> He signed a two-year contract with Scottish club [[St Mirren F.C.|St Mirren]] in May 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/44215783|title=JJames Kellermann: St Mirren sign midfielder from Aldershot Town|date=23 May 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018|publisher=BC Sport}}</ref> He made his debut for St Mirren on 13 July 2018 in the [[Scottish League Cup]].<ref>{{soccerbase season|94096|2018|accessdate=12 July 2018}}</ref> He moved on loan to [[AFC Fylde]] in August 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.afcfylde.co.uk/jim-kellermann-signs-on-loan/|title=JIM KELLERMANN SIGNS ON LOAN|date=17 August 2018|accessdate=17 August 2018|publisher=AFC Fylde}}</ref> He left St Mirren by mutual consent on 29 October 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/sport/football/st-mirren-midfielder-reaches-agreement-20751390|title=St Mirren midfielder reaches agreement with club to end contract early|first=Ben|last=Ramage|date=29 October 2019|website=dailyrecord}}</ref>

In March 2020 he signed for [[Kidderminster Harriers F.C.|Kidderminster Harriers]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://harriers-online.co.uk/khfc/09-03-20-news-the-harriers-bring-in-a-new-midfielder/|title=09-03-20 - News - The Harriers bring in a new midfielder|date=9 March 2020}}</ref>
