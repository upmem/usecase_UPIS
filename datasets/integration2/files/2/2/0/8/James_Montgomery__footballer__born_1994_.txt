'''James Hayden Montgomery''' (born 20 April 1994) is an English professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Gateshead F.C.|Gateshead]] as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]].

==Early and personal life==
Born in [[Sunderland]],<ref name = "SW">{{soccerway|james-montgomery/308385|accessdate=3 April 2020}}</ref> his grandfather was a cousin of footballer [[Jimmy Montgomery]].<ref name = "FGR"/>

==Career==
Montgomery began his career playing youth football for [[Middlesbrough F.C.|Middlesbrough]].<ref name = "FGR"/> He then played in non-league with [[Guiseley A.F.C.|Guiseley]], before attending the [[Nike Football Academy]] and signing with [[A.F.C. Telford United|AFC Telford United]] in 2015.<ref name = "CL">{{cite web|url=https://www.chroniclelive.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/sunderland-afc-keeper-james-montgomery-11634118|title=Sunderland AFC: Keeper James Montgomery - a relative of Jim - is training with the Black Cat|date=19 July 2016|accessdate=24 May 2018|author=Steve Brown|publisher=ChronicleLive}}</ref> While with Telford he also began training with [[Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland]] (in July 2016),<ref name = "CL"/> and  with the V9 Academy (in November 2016).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gazettelive.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/former-middlesbrough-goalkeeper-signs-jamie-12251034|title=Former Middlesbrough goalkeeper signs for Jamie Vardy's V9 Academy|date=30 November 2016|accessdate=24 May 2018|author=Adam Steel|publisher=GazetteLive}}</ref> He moved to [[Gateshead F.C.|Gateshead]] in January 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/38797953|title=James Montgomery: Gateshead sign AFC Telford United goalkeeper|date=30 January 2017|accessdate=24 May 2018|publisher=BBC Sport}}</ref> Montgomery signed a two-year contract with [[Forest Green Rovers F.C.|Forest Green Rovers]] in May 2018.<ref name = "FGR">{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/44228936|title=James Montgomery: Forest Green sign Gateshead goalkeeper|date=23 May 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018|publisher=BBC Sport}}</ref> He made his professional debut on 14 August 2018, in the [[EFL Cup]].<ref>{{soccerbase season|81739|2018|accessdate=15 August 2018}}</ref> During a match against [[Mansfield Town F.C.|Mansfield Town]] in January 2019, Montgomery collided with opposition player [[Gethin Jones (footballer, born 1995)|Gethin Jones]], resulting in a cut lip and lost teeth. Montgomery was substituted by [[Lewis Ward (footballer)|Lewis Ward]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/47054699|title=Forest Green: James Montgomery loses teeth in collision with Mansfield player's boot|date=30 January 2019|accessdate=30 January 2019|publisher=BBC Sport}}</ref>

In July 2019, Montgomery joined [[AFC Fylde]] on a two-year deal.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.fgr.co.uk/news/july-2019/news-goalkeeper-montgomery-joins-afc-fylde |title=Goalkeeper Montgomery joins AFC Fylde |date=23 July 2019 |publisher=Forest Green Rovers F.C. |language=en |access-date=3 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.afcfylde.co.uk/welcome-james-craigen-montgomery-join-coasters/ |title=WELCOME JAMES - Craigen & Montgomery Join Coasters |date=23 July 2019 |publisher=AFC Fylde |language=en-GB |access-date=3 April 2020}}</ref> He was released by the club in August 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.blackpoolgazette.co.uk/sport/football/afc-fylde-sign-winger-junior-mondal-and-release-keeper-james-montgomery-2958806|title=AFC Fylde sign winger Junior Mondal and release keeper James Montgomery|website=www.blackpoolgazette.co.uk}}</ref>

He returned to Gateshead in October 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gateshead-fc.com/news/montgomery-returns-2574090.html|title=StackPath|website=www.gateshead-fc.com}}</ref>
