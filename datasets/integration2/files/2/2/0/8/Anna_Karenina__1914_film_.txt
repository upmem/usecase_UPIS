'''''Anna Karenina''''' ({{lang-ru|Анна Каренина}}) is a 1914 Russian drama film directed and written by [[Vladimir Gardin]].<ref>[https://www.filmpro.ru/materials/38001 Елизавета Боярская сыграет Анну Каренину]</ref><ref>[http://ru.vpoltave.info/post/47642 123 года назад родился один из самых известных полтавских киноактеров]{{Dead link|date=September 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>[https://ria.ru/spravka/20130805/953901913.html Биография Веры Холодной]</ref><ref>[https://rian.com.ua/culture_society/20130805/338156436.html Исполняется 120 лет со дня рождения актрисы немого кино Веры Холодной]</ref>

== Plot ==
The film is based on [[Anna Karenina|the 1877 novel]] by [[Leo Tolstoy]].

== Cast ==
* [[Maria Germanova]] as Anna Karenina
* Vladimir Shaternikov as Karenin
* Mikhail Tamarov as Vronsky
* [[Zoya Barantsevich]] as Kittie
* V. Obolensky as Levin
* Vladimir Kvanin as Stiva Oblonsky
* Moreva as Dolly
* [[Vera Kholodnaya]] as Italian wet-nurse (uncredited)<ref>[https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0003625/fullcredits/?ref_=tt_ov_st_sm Анна Каренина (1914) Full Cast & Crew]</ref>
