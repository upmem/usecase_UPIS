'''Antonio Horvath''' (3 January 1950 – 23 May 2018) was a Chilean politician who served as a [[Chamber of Deputies of Chile|Deputy]] from 1990 to 1994, and as a [[Senate of Chile|Senator]] from 1994 to 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.latercera.com/politica/noticia/fallece-ex-senador-antonio-horvath/175769/|title=Fallece ex senador Antonio Horvath|date=23 May 2018|website=La Tercera|accessdate=3 June 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bcn.cl/historiapolitica|title=Biblioteca del Congreso Nacional - Historia Política|first=Biblioteca del Congreso|last=Nacional|website=bcn.cl|accessdate=3 June 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.elmostrador.cl/noticias/pais/2018/05/23/ex-senador-antonio-horvath-fallece-a-los-68-anos/|title=Murió Antonio Horvath: el ex senador que puso de cabeza a Piñera por Hidroaysén|first=El|last=Mostrador|date=23 May 2018|publisher=|accessdate=3 June 2019}}</ref><ref>https://radio.uchile.cl/2018/05/23/muere-ex-senador-antonio-horvath-producto-de-un-cancer-linfatico/</ref>
