'''Sungji Hong''' ({{lang-ko|홍성지}}) is a [[South Korea]]n [[composer]]. Her work ''Missa Lumen de Lumine'', a setting of the Roman-rite Catholic Mass ordinary for three voices, was written for [[Trio Mediæval]] in 2002.{{r|all}} The trio recorded it in 2005. In a review for [[Gramophone (magazine)|''The Gramophone'']], [[Ivan Moody (composer)|Ivan Moody]] found it "rather a fragmentary affair", though with "some dazzling moments".{{r|ivor}}
