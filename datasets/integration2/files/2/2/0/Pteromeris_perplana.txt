'''''Pteromeris perplana''''', or the flat cardita, is a species of [[bivalve]] [[mollusc]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Carditidae]]. It can be found along the Atlantic coast of North America, ranging from [[North Carolina]] to [[Florida]].<ref>Abbott, R.T. & Morris, P.A. ''A Field Guide to Shells: Atlantic and Gulf Coasts and the West Indies.'' New York: Houghton Mifflin, 1995. 45.</ref>
