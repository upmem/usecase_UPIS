'''Grądzik''' {{IPAc-pl|'|g|r|o|n|dź|i|k}} ({{lang-de|Grünhöfchen}})<ref>{{cite book| title=Ortsnamenverzeichnis der Ortschaften jenseits von Oder u. Neiße |author=M. Kaemmerer|year=2004| ISBN= 3-7921-0368-0 |language=German}}</ref>is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|14|km|mi|0}} north-east of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|19|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|66|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].
