'''Camp Mont Shenandoah''' is an all-girls summer camp near [[Millboro Springs, Virginia]].  Located on {{convert|60|acre|ha}} in the Allegheny Mountains of western Virginia, it was established in 1927, and is one of the oldest continuously-operating summer camps in the state.  Its buildings and grounds are mainly in a rustic style befitting the environment.  In addition to residential cabins, it has a lodge and dining hall.  Activities supported include tennis, basketball, canoeing on the [[Cowpasture River]], and archery, as well as arts and crafts.<ref name=NRHP>{{cite web|url=http://www.dhr.virginia.gov/registers/Counties/Bath/008-5048_CampMontShenandoah_2014_NRHP_FINAL.pdf|title=NRHP nomination for Camp Mont Shenandoah Historic District|publisher=Virginia DHR|accessdate=2015-09-19}}</ref>  The camp was listed on the [[National Register of Historic Places]] in 2015.<ref name="nris"/>
