'''''Camper Lifelovers''''' is a [[Volvo Open 70]] yacht. She finished second in the [[2011–12 Volvo Ocean Race]] skippered by [[Chris Nicholson (sailor)|Chris Nicholson]].

==Career==
''Camper Lifelovers'' was designed by Emirates Team New Zealand design team and built at [[Cookson Boats]] in New Zealand for Camper with [[Team New Zealand]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mysailing.com.au/news/volvo-ocean-race-camper-rolls-out-of-cookson-s-shed-in-auckland|title=My Sailing: Volvo Ocean Race: Camper rolls out of Cookson's shed in Auckland|work=mysailing.com.au}}</ref> It got the name ''Camper Lifelovers''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://footwearnews.com/2011/business/news/mediabank-adidas-turf-war-camper-sets-sail-63501/|title=MediaBank: Adidas’ Turf War… Camper Sets Sail… - Footwear News|author=Kristen Henning|work=Footwear News}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://old.revistametal.com/2011/09/2724/|title=CAMPER LIFELOVERS:|author=yolanda|work=revistametal.com|access-date=2015-09-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305211418/http://old.revistametal.com/2011/09/2724/|archive-date=2016-03-05|url-status=dead}}</ref> The graphic elements of the boat was designed by [[Mark Farrow]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.camper.com/en_SE/volvo-ocean-race/the-boat|title=Camper in the Volvo Ocean Race - The Boat|work=camper.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://desktopmag.com.au/project-wall/camper-in-the-volvo-ocean-race/#.Vf6S-7QkK2w|title=CAMPER in the Volvo Ocean Race - Desktop|work=DesktopMag}}</ref>

The yacht competed in the [[2011–12 Volvo Ocean Race]] skippered by [[Chris Nicholson (sailor)|Chris Nicholson]], where she finished second after ''[[Groupama 4]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sailing.org/volvo-ocean-race-2011-12.php?view=eventoa&rgtaid=10019&regattaid=10019&evntid=18972#results__10019|title=Events - ISAF - World Sailing - Official Website : Volvo Ocean Race 2011-2012|work=sailing.org}}</ref>
