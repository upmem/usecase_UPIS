'''Sojasun''' is a [[Brittany]]-based [[France|French]] company created in 1988 that sells [[soy]]-based products. The soy is 100% produced in France. The company is part of the {{ill|Triballat Noyal|fr}} company, which was founded in 1874.

The brand has about 50 different soja products in 5 different categories. About 17% of French people buy its products.<ref>http://www.entreprises.gouv.fr/semaine-industrie/visite-la-societe-sojasun</ref>
