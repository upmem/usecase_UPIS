'''Richards High School''' is a [[public high school]] located in [[Richards, Texas|Richards]], [[Texas]] ([[United States|USA]]) and classified as a 1A school by the [[University Interscholastic League|UIL]]. It is a part of the [[Richards Independent School District]] located in south central [[Grimes County, Texas|Grimes County]]. In 2015, the school was rated "[[Texas Education Agency accountability ratings system|Met Standard]]" by the [[Texas Education Agency]].<ref>{{cite web|url = http://ritter.tea.state.tx.us/perfreport/account/2015/campusbytype.pdf|title = 2015 Accountability Rating System|publisher = Texas Education Agency|url-status = dead|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150910233604/http://ritter.tea.state.tx.us/perfreport/account/2015/campusbytype.pdf|archivedate = 2015-09-10}}</ref>
