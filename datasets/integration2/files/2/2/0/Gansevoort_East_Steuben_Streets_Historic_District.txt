'''Gansevoort/East Steuben Streets Historic District''' is a national [[Historic district (United States)|historic district]] located at [[Bath (village), New York|Bath]] in [[Steuben County, New York]]. The district contains 22 residences dating from about 1830 to 1908 and designed in a broad range of architectural styles.<ref name="nrhpinv_ny">{{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=8501|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: Gansevoort/East Steuben Streets Historic District|date=March 1983|accessdate=2009-10-26 |author=Division for Historic Preservation|publisher=[[New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation]]}} ''See also:'' {{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=8502|title=Accompanying five photos}}</ref>

It was listed on the [[National Register of Historic Places]] in 1983.<ref name="nris"/>
