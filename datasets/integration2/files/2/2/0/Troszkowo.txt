'''Troszkowo''' {{IPAc-pl|t|r|o|sz|'|k|o|w|o}} ({{lang-de|Klackendorf}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Bisztynek]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|22|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south of [[Bartoszyce]] and {{convert|41|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].
