'''Grotowo''' {{IPAc-pl|g|r|o|'|t|o|w|o}} ({{lang-de|Hoppendorf}})<ref name="Former Territory of Germany">{{cite web |url=http://gov.genealogy.net/item/show/HOPORFKO04DH |title=Former Territory of Germany|date=2017-11-08 |language=German}}</ref> is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|11|km|mi|0}} west of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|32|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|58|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].

The village has an approximate population of 80.
