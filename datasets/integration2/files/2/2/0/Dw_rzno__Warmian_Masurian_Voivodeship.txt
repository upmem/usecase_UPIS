'''Dwórzno''' {{IPAc-pl|'|d|w|u|ż|n|o}} ({{lang-de|Hoofe}})<ref name="Former Territory of Germany">{{cite web |url=http://gov.genealogy.net/item/show/HOOOFEKO04FG |title=Former Territory of Germany|date=2017-11-08 |language=German}}</ref> is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|3|km|mi|0}} south-west of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|22|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|52|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].

Hoofe was the scene of the "Bataille de Hoff" throughout the Napoleonic [[Battle of Eylau]] in February 1807.<ref>[http://gustave.club.fr/LGA/batailles/Hoff.pdf Bataille de Hoff] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110720212146/http://gustave.club.fr/LGA/batailles/Hoff.pdf |date=2011-07-20 }} (French)</ref>
