'''Nerqin Khotanan''' ({{lang-hy|Ներքին Խոտանան}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Nerk’in Khotanan''' and '''Nerkin Khotanan'''; Russified as '''Nizhniy Khotanan''') is a village and [[Community (Armenia)|rural community]] (municipality) in the [[Syunik Region|Syunik Province]] of [[Armenia]].  The [[National Statistical Service of the Republic of Armenia]] (ARMSTAT) reported its population was 70 in 2010,<ref>{{ARMSTATdata|44}}</ref> down from 101 at the 2001 census.<ref>{{ArmenianCensus|210}}</ref>
