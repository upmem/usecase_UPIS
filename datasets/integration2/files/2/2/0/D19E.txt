The '''D19E''', also known as '''Đổi mới''',<ref name="vnrolling">{{cite web|url=http://www.vr.com.vn/English/hientai_hoatdong_daumay.htm |title=Vietnam Railways: Rolling Stock |accessdate=2011-01-20 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110113103821/http://www.vr.com.vn/English/hientai_hoatdong_daumay.htm |archivedate=2011-01-13 }} {{in lang|vi}}</ref> is a series of [[diesel locomotive]]s currently used on the [[Vietnam]]ese railway network. 

==History==
The series is one of the most recently acquired by state railway company [[Vietnam Railways]]; 40 were in service as of 2005,<ref name="vnrolling" /> 80 in 2012.<ref name="railwaysinvietnam">[http://www.railwaysinvietnam.com/D19E.html D19E -"Đổi Mới"], railwaysinvietnam.com</ref> The series, of the diesel electric type [[China Railways CKD7F]], was constructed by [[People's Republic of China|Chinese]] manufacturer [[CSR Ziyang Locomotive Co., Ltd.]].

No. 901 to 920 (built 2003—2004) and 921 to 940 (built 2006—2007) have a more angular body. No. 941 to 960 (built 2007—2008) were built with a rounded front; in No. 961 to 980 (built 2011—2012) the windows and headlights were modified and the front air intake omitted.<ref name="railwaysinvietnam" /> From No. 941 the final assembly took place at the [[Gia Lâm District|Gia Lâm]] works of Vietnam Railways.

==Accidents and incidents==
{{main|Dien Sanh train crash}}
On 10 March 2015 locomotive №968 was written off in an accident near Dien Sanh when it pulled SE5 train was in collision with a [[lorry]] on a [[level crossing]].

On May 24, 2018 locomotive No 927 towing the SE19 carrying 400 passengers had an accident in Thanh Hoa because of a collision with a truck, causing two deaths: driving the train Nguyen The Hung (born 1976) and Nguyen Xuan De (born 1985) The accident investigated was caused by two guards who forgot to close the barriers.
