"'''Es Por Ti'''" (English: ''Because of You'') is a song written and performed by Colombian singer-songwriter [[Juanes]]. It's the second radio single from his sophomore studio album ''[[Un Día Normal]]'' (2002). It was released on September 23, 2002 (see [[2002 in music]]).

The single earned him two [[Latin Grammy Awards]] for [[Latin Grammy Award for Song of the Year|Song of the Year]] and [[Latin Grammy Award for Record of the Year|Record of the Year]].<ref name=LatinGrammy3>{{cite web|url= http://www.grammy.com/nominees/latin/search?artist=&title=&year=All&genre=45|title=Past Winners Search|publisher=[[National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences]]|accessdate=July 11, 2011}}</ref> The song is a slow-rock pop ballad that revolves around love towards a romantic partner.
