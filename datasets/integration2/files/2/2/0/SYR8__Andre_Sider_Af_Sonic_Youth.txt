'''''SYR8: Andre Sider af Sonic Youth''''' is a live album by [[Sonic Youth]] with [[Mats Gustafsson]] and [[Merzbow]]. It was the eighth release in the [[Sonic Youth Recordings|SYR]] series. It was released on July 28, 2008. The album was recorded on July 1, [[Roskilde Festival 2005|2005]] at the [[Roskilde Festival]]. The album title is in [[Danish language|Danish]] and means "Other sides of Sonic Youth".
