'''Aknaghbyur''' ({{lang-hy|Ակնաղբյուր}}; until 1967, '''Nerkin Agdan''' and '''Nerkin Aghdan''', Russified as '''Nizhniy Agdan''', from 1967 to 1970, '''Morut''') is a town in the [[Tavush|Tavush Province]] of [[Armenia]]. Aknaghbyur was founded in 451 in honor of [[Vartan Mamikonian]] and is one of Armenia's oldest rural communities.<ref name="ArmeniaFund"/> In recent years, [[ArmeniaFund]] has launched and completed several vital projects on the infrastructure of the town, such as the construction of a gas pipeline and an irrigation system and the renovation of the drinking-water system.<ref name="ArmeniaFund"/>
