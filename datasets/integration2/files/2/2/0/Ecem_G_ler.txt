'''Emel Ecem Güler''' (born 16 January 1992 in [[Soma, Manisa|Soma]], [[Turkey]]) is a Turkish [[basketball]] player. She plays for [[Fenerbahçe İstanbul]] as both small and power forward position. She is 187&nbsp;cm tall and 75&nbsp;kg weights. She has played for Fenerbahçe İstanbul since 2004 in youth level and since 2010 in senior level.

==Honors==
*'''Turkish Championship''' (3): 2010-11, 2011–12, 2012–13
*'''Turkish Presidents Cup''' (1): 2010
