'''Heikki Liimatainen''', known professionally as '''Heikki L,'''  is a [[house music]] [[record producer|producer]], [[DJ]] and [[remix]]er<ref name="Inc.2008">{{cite news|title=Hot Dance Airplay|newspaper=Billboard|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=DhMEAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA61|accessdate=17 April 2011|date=2008-06-14|publisher=Nielsen Business Media, Inc.|page=61}}</ref> from [[Finland]].

==Biography==
Heikki Liimatainen has released music under different aliases like Heikki L, Supermodels From Paris, Syndicate of Beats, [[Dallas Superstars]], Modulation, Moovon, Spectro, Hemohes and Bostik. Under his current alias, Heikki L, he has produced many big house tracks and also remixed many artists. Liimatainen has his own studio in Helsinki and also runs Finland's biggest House Club, Danceteria with his partner Jere Hyv√∂nen.
Heikki L is also a DJ and he has played together with DJs like [[Erick Morillo]], [[Axwell]], [[Sebastian Ingrosso]].
