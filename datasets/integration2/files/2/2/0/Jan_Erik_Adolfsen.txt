'''Jan Erik Adolfsen''' (June 1, 1930 &ndash; March 27, 2001) was a Norwegian [[ice hockey]] player. He played for the [[Norway men's national ice hockey team|Norwegian national ice hockey team]], and  participated at the [[Winter Olympics]] in 1952, where the Norwegian team placed 9th.<ref name=sref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ad/jan-erik-adolfsen-1.html |title=Jan Erik Adolfsen |publisher=Sports-reference.com |accessdate=6 April 2011 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121105104008/http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ad/jan-erik-adolfsen-1.html |archivedate= 5 November 2012 }}</ref>
