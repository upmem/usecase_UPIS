'''Księżno''' {{IPAc-pl|'|k|ś|ę|ż|n|o}} ({{lang-de|Fürstenau}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Bisztynek]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|5|km|mi|0}} south-west of [[Bisztynek]], {{convert|23|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|37|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].
