'''Pleśnik''' {{IPAc-pl|'|p|l|e|ś|n|i|k}} ({{lang-de|Plößenhof}})<ref name="Former Territory of Germany">{{cite web|url=http://gov.genealogy.net/item/show/PLOHOFKO04MB|title=Former Territory of Germany|date=2017-11-08|language=German}}</ref> is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Bisztynek]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|12|km|mi|0}} east of [[Bisztynek]], {{convert|25|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-east of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|51|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].

The village has a population of 92.
