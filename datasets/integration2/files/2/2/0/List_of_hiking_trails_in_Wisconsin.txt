This is a really limited list of [[long-distance trails|hiking trails]] in the [[U.S. state]] of [[Wisconsin]]. The list does not include many smaller trails that are found in places such as [[List of Wisconsin state parks|Wisconsin state parks]], and it lists biking trails as hiking trails.
