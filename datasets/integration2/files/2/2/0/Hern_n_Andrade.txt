'''Hernán Andrade Yañez''' (born November 1, 1960) is a retired [[racewalker]] from [[Mexico]].

He competed, but was disqualified, in the 50 kilometres races at the [[1987 IAAF World Race Walking Cup|1987 World Race Walking Cup]] [[Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Men's 50 km walk|1988 Olympic Games]]. He competed at the [[1991 IAAF World Race Walking Cup|1991 World Race Walking Cup]], but did not finish.

He set his personal best (3:49:38) in the men's 50&nbsp;km walk event in 1988.

Competitions :

World Cup Race Walking in San Jose California 1991

Olympic Games South Korea 1988

Panamerican Race Walking Cup Mar de Plata, Argentina 1988

Panamerican Race Walking Cup Bucaramanga, Colombia 1984

Track and Field Centro American Games Havana, Cuba 1982

Semana Internacional de Caminata second place 50k 1987

Semana Internacional de Caminata Best Personal Time 3:48:34 1988 D.F México

Andrade is currently living in Quintana Roo, as a tourism promoter
