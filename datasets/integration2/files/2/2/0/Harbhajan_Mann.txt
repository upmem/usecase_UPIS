'''Harbhajan Mann''' (born 30 December 1965) is an [[Indo-Canadians|Indian-Canadian]] singer, actor and film producer associated with Punjabi music and cinema.<ref name = "db"/><ref name="ie">{{cite news|author=[[Amrita Chaudhry]]|title=Harbhajan Mann to perform at Winter Olympiad|url=http://www.indianexpress.com/news/harbhajan-mann-to-perform-at-winter-olympiad/523556/|accessdate=11 September 2010|newspaper=[[The Indian Express]]|date=1 October 2009|archive-date=11 October 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201011142823/http://archive.indianexpress.com/news/harbhajan-mann-to-perform-at-winter-olympiad/523556|url-status=live}}</ref> His movies include ''[[Jee Aayan Nu]] (2002)'', ''Asa Nu Mann Watnan Da (2002)'', ''Heer Ranjha (2009)'' and ''[[Jag Jeondeyan De Mele]] (2009)''.

==Career==
He was born in Khemuana village, [[Bathinda]] district of [[Punjab, India|Punjab]], India.<ref>{{cite web|last=Ballewood|title=Facts about Harbhajan Mann|url=http://ballewood.in/celebrities/star-features/harbhajan-mann|publisher=Ballewood.in|accessdate=16 August 2013|archive-date=20 August 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130820081832/http://ballewood.in/celebrities/star-features/harbhajan-mann|url-status=dead}}</ref> Mann began singing as an amateur in 1980 and performed in local shows for the South Asian community while attending high school in Canada.{{citation needed|date=November 2012}} His beginnings as a professional artist can be traced to 1992, he was in [[Punjab region|Punjab]]. It was then that his recording of ''Chithiye Nee Chithiye'' was released and this track became a regional success.{{citation needed|date=November 2012}} Mann realized that the market for Punjabi music in Canada was small and he returned to Punjab to record his albums.

Mann had a breakthrough in 1999 when exposure provided by India [[MTV]] and [[T-Series (company)|T-Series]] assisted his ''Oye Hoye'' album to become a success.{{citation needed|date=November 2012}} His Punjabi-pop style soon attracted audiences from all over India and he also undertook [[playback singing]] roles where his voice was [[Dubbing (filmmaking)|dubbed]] on to those of movie actors.{{citation needed|date=November 2012}}

The playback work led to acting roles and Mann has become a prominent figure in the revitalisation of [[Punjabi cinema]]. He has starred and produced in seven movies – ''[[Ji Aayan Nu]]'', ''Asa Nu Mann Watna Da'', ''[[Dil Apna Punjabi]]'', ''[[Mitti Wajaan Mardi]]'', ''[[Mera Pind-My home]]'', ''Jag Jeondiyan De Mele'' and his most recent movie,{{when|date=November 2012}} ''Heer Ranjha''.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sify.com/news/Punjabi-actor-Gaganchahal-auctions-39-Heer-Ranjha-39-costumes-news-jlmuEcbidbe.html|title=Punjabi actor Harbhajan Mann auctions 'Heer Ranjha' costumes|date=12 November 2009|newspaper=[[Sify]]|accessdate=11 September 2010|archive-date=15 November 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161115131906/http://www.sify.com/news/Punjabi-actor-Gaganchahal-auctions-39-Heer-Ranjha-39-costumes-news-jlmuEcbidbe.html|url-status=live}}</ref>

On 2 January 2013, he released ''Satrangi Peengh 2'' along with his brother [[Gursewak Mann]]. Harbhajan Mann said that he wants to create music that will live for decades.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.punjabiportal.com/articles/satrangi-peengh-2-harbhajan-mann |title=Harbhajan Mann and Gursewak Mann performed Satrangi Peengh 2 for Press |work=Punjabiportal.com |date=5 January 2012 |access-date=5 January 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130107061436/http://www.punjabiportal.com/articles/satrangi-peengh-2-harbhajan-mann |archive-date=7 January 2013 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

In 2013, Mann acted in Hanni which was directed by [[Amitoj Maan]]. The two have worked together again in Punjabi film [[Gaddar: The Traitor|''Gaddar – The Traitor'']], which was released on 29 May 2015.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.punjabupfilms.in/gadaar-the-traitor-trailer-released-full-movie-releasing-on-29th-may/ |title=Archived copy |access-date=2 June 2015 |archive-date=28 April 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150428224443/http://www.punjabupfilms.in/gadaar-the-traitor-trailer-released-full-movie-releasing-on-29th-may/ |url-status=live }}</ref><ref name="Punjabiportal.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.punjabiportal.com/articles/gaddar-harbhajan-mann-punjabi-movie|title=Gaddar – Harbhajan Mann & Amitoj Maan|publisher=Punjabiportal.com|accessdate=12 October 2014|archive-date=19 October 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141019044744/http://www.punjabiportal.com/articles/gaddar-harbhajan-mann-punjabi-movie|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Harbhajan Mann released his single "Delhi '84" in 2014 for which the music has been given by [[Sukshinder Shinda]].<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eF27jveCcOo= | title = Delhi 1984 "Harbhajan Mann" | publisher = Youtube | access-date = 18 August 2020 | archive-date = 11 October 2020 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20201011142837/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eF27jveCcOo | url-status = live }}</ref>
