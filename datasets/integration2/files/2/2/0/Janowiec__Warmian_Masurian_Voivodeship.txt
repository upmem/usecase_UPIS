'''Janowiec''' {{IPAc-pl|j|a|'|n|o|w|j|e|c}} ({{lang-de|Johannisruhe}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Bisztynek]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref>

[[First Partition of Poland|Before 1772]] the area was part of [[Crown of the Kingdom of Poland|Kingdom of Poland]], 1772-1945 Prussia and Germany ([[East Prussia]]).
