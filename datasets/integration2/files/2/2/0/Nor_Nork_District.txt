'''Nor Nork''' ({{lang-hy|Նոր Նորք}}, ''Nor Nork'') is one of the [[Districts of Yerevan|12 districts]] of [[Yerevan]], the capital of [[Armenia]]. It is located at the eastern part of the city. It is bordered by the districts of [[Nork-Marash District|Nork-Marash]], [[Kentron District|Kentron]] and [[Kanaker-Zeytun District|Kanaker-Zeytun]] from the west, [[Avan District|Avan]] from the north and [[Erebuni District|Erebuni]] from the south. [[Kotayk Province]] forms the eastern border of the district.<ref>[https://www.yerevan.am/am/districts/nor-nork/ Nor Nork at Yerevan.am]</ref>

The district is unofficially divided into smaller neighborhoods including the 9 blocks of Nor Nork and the Bagrevand neighborhood. As of the 2011 census, the population of the district is 126,065.
