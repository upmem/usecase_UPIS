'''Pieszkowo''' {{IPAc-pl|p|j|e|sz|'|k|o|w|o}} ({{lang-de|Petershagen}})<ref name="Former Territory of Germany">{{cite web|url=http://gov.genealogy.net/item/show/PETGENKO04GF|title=Former Territory of Germany|date=2017-11-08|language=German}}</ref> is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|6|km|mi|0}} south of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|20|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|48|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany ([[East Prussia]]).

* [[Klaas Reimer]] (1770–1837) the founder of the [[Kleine Gemeinde]], a [[Mennonite]] denomination
