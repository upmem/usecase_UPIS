'''Kristian Dacey''' (born 25 July 1989) is a [[Wales|Welsh]] [[rugby union]] player. A hooker, he plays club rugby for the  [[Cardiff Blues]] having started his rugby playing with [[Merthyr RFC]] before honing his skills at Pontypridd RFC.

On 20 January 2015, Dacey was named in the 34-man [[Wales national rugby union team|Wales]] squad for the [[2015 Six Nations Championship]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wru.co.uk/eng/news/32454.php#.VL43f02p7IU|title=Wales name 34-man Six Nations squad|work=WRU|date=20 January 2015}}</ref>

Dacey made his full international debut for Wales versus Ireland on 8 August 2015 as a second-half replacement.

Dacey was called into the British and Irish Lions as a Squad player but didn't get any game time.
