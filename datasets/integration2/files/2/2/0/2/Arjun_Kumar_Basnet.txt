'''Arjun Kumar Basnet''' ({{lang-ne|अर्जुन कुमार बस्नेत}}) (born December 5, 1975) is a [[Nepal]]ese marathon runner.<ref>{{cite sports-reference|title = Arjun Kumar Basnet|url = https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ba/arjun-kumar-basnet-1.html|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20200418074512/https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ba/arjun-kumar-basnet-1.html|url-status = dead|archive-date = 18 April 2020|access-date = 11 December 2012}}</ref> Basnet represented [[Nepal]] at the [[2008 Summer Olympics]] in [[Beijing]], where he competed for the [[Athletics at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Men's marathon|men's marathon]]. He finished the race in forty-fifth place by approximately quarter of a minute (about 26 seconds) behind South Africa's [[Hendrick Ramaala]], with his personal best time of 2:23:09.<ref>{{cite web|title=Men's Marathon |url=http://www.2008.nbcolympics.com/trackandfield/resultsandschedules/rsc=ATM099100/standings.html |publisher=[[NBC Olympics]] |accessdate=11 December 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120816164758/http://www.2008.nbcolympics.com/trackandfield/resultsandschedules/rsc%3DATM099100/standings.html |archivedate=16 August 2012 }}</ref>
