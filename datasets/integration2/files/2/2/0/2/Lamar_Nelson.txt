'''Lamar Delmarro Nelson''' is a Jamaican footballer who plays for [[Arnett Gardens F.C.]] in the top-flight [[National Premier League|Jamaica National Premier League]]. He is also known as "Wanka".<ref>{{cite web|last=Walker|first=Howard|title='Junglists' excited over rising star Wanka|url=http://www.jamaicaobserver.com/sport/-Junglists--excited-over-rising-star-Wanka_12652360|publisher=Jamaica Observer|accessdate=11 December 2012|date=30 September 2012}}</ref>

== Club career ==

Nelson started his senior career at [[Arnett Gardens F.C.]].

In 2013, Nelson signed with Waterhouse FC in the RSPL.<ref>{{cite news | url=http://jamaica-gleaner.com/gleaner/20130820/sports/sports5.html | title='Wonka' transfers to Waterhouse FC | date=20 August 2013 | newspaper=The Gleaner | location=Jamaica | first=Marc | last=Stamp | accessdate=9 March 2020 }}</ref> After an extended layoff due to knee surgery, in June 2015, Nelson returned to Arnett Gardens.<ref>http://www.jamaicaobserver.com/sport/Injured-Lamar-Nelson-needs--750-000-for-career-saving-knee-surgery-soon_18129228</ref><ref>http://jamaica-gleaner.com/article/sports/20150609/lamar-wonka-nelson-back-arnett-gardens-after-surgery</ref>

== International career ==

He made his international début for Jamaica in a 0–0 with Martinique on 10 December 2012 in the [[2012 Caribbean Cup]].
