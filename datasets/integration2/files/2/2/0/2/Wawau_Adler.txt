'''Josef Wawau Adler''' (born 25 January 1967, in [[Karlsruhe]]) is a German gypsy jazz guitarist.<ref name="Dregni2008">{{cite book|last=Dregni|first=Michael|title=Gypsy Jazz:In Search of Django Reinhardt and the Soul of Gypsy Swing: In Search of Django Reinhardt and the Soul of Gypsy Swing|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=smcDVbulh1gC&pg=PA323|accessdate=11 December 2012|date=4 April 2008|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-531192-1|page=323}}</ref> Born into a family of [[Sinti]] gypsies, he is heavily influenced by [[Django Reinhardt]], but also [[Wes Montgomery]], [[Charlie Parker]], [[Pat Martino]] and [[George Benson]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hyperhipmedia.com/HM0017.html|title=In the Style of Wawau Adler|publisher=Hyperhipmedia.com|accessdate=11 December 2012}}</ref> He formed a trio with [[Holzmanno Winterstein]] and [[Axel Miller]],<ref name="Südtiroler Wochenmagazin">{{cite book|title=Südtiroler Wochenmagazin|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=YSkjAQAAIAAJ|accessdate=11 December 2012|date=June 2006|publisher=FF, die Südtiroler Illustrierte|page=50}}</ref> and has also played with [[Andreas Öberg]] and [[Joel Locher]], the bassist who he released ''Here’s to Django'' with in 2010, and many others.
