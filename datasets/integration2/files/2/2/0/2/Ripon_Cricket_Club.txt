'''Ripon Cricket Club''' are an English club [[cricket]] side based in the medieval city of [[Ripon]], [[North Yorkshire]]. Formed in 1810 the club now plays in the York & District Senior Cricket League.

==History==
Ripon Cricket Club is one of the oldest clubs in [[Yorkshire]] and boasts a rich heritage. The club has played host to a number of renowned touring sides and the clubs Studley Road ground is used frequently by the Yorkshire Second XI<ref>{{cite web|title=Ripon Cricket Club Website|url=http://www.riponcc.co.uk/|publisher=Ripon Cricket Club}}</ref> for their home fixtures.

===Famous Players===
Ripon boasts many players who have gone onto play cricket at the highest standard including the current club president [[Peter Squires]] who played first class cricket for [[Yorkshire County Cricket Club]] as well as representing [[England national rugby union team|England]] twenty nine times in Rugby Union, scoring six tries.<ref>{{cite web|title=Rugby Union - Players & Officials Databse|url=http://www.espnscrum.com/england/rugby/player/8025.html|publisher=ESPN}}</ref>

===Popularity===
The club fields three senior teams, a 1st XI who play in the York & District Senior League,<ref>{{cite web|title=York & District Senior Cricket League Team Directory|url=http://www.yorkercricket.co.uk/Clubs/Ripon/Directory.html|publisher=York & District Senior Cricket League}}</ref> a 2nd XI & 3rd XI who play in the Nidderdale League<ref>{{cite web|title=Theakston's Nidderdale League|url=http://www.nidderdaleleague.co.uk/|publisher=Nidderdale League}}</ref> and an evening league side who play in the Harrogate and District Evening League.<ref>{{cite web|title=Halifax and District Evening League League Tables|url=http://www.freewebs.com/hgtevelge/2012leaguetables.htm|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130123140323/http://www.freewebs.com/hgtevelge/2012leaguetables.htm|url-status=dead|archive-date=2013-01-23|publisher=Harrogate and District Evening League}}</ref>

The club also fields a strong junior setup featuring four junior teams. It also provides coaching for both boys and girls from age seven upwards.
