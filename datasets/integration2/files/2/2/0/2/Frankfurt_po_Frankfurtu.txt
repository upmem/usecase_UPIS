The '''Frankfurt After The Frankfurt''' ({{lang-sl|Frankfurt po Frankfurtu}}) is the traditional [[Slovenia]]n foreign books [[trade fair]] held each year in November at Mladinska knjiga, Bookstore Konzorcij, where a selection of the books published by foreign publishers and presented at [[Frankfurt Book Fair]] (Germany) the same year is presented and sold.<ref name="Dnevnik_FpF_2012">[http://www.dnevnik.si/kultura/letosnji-frankfurt-po-frankfurtu-v-konzorcij-prinasa-francijo-in-slavoja-zizka This year's the FpF brings France and Žižek] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130423105219/http://www.dnevnik.si/kultura/letosnji-frankfurt-po-frankfurtu-v-konzorcij-prinasa-francijo-in-slavoja-zizka |date=2013-04-23 }}, Dnevnik, Ljubljana, 12 November.</ref>

In 2018, it will be held in Cankarjev dom from 20. till 25. November.
