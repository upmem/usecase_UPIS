'''Tomas Arthwr Davies''' (born 24 March 1986) is a [[rugby union]] player. A [[Rugby union positions#Prop|prop forward]], he played club rugby for [[Doncaster R.F.C.|Doncaster Knights]] from 2005 to 2015,<ref name=drfc>[http://www.drfc.co.uk/news/davies-to-start-in-castle-park-bow/ www.drfc.co.uk April 17, 2015] Davies hoping to bow out on a high.</ref> having previously played for [[Cardiff Blues]]. Davies qualifies for [[Wales]] as both his parents were born in [[Aberdare]] and he represented Wales at Under 18 level<ref name=Whitchurch>[http://www.pitchero.com/clubs/whitchurchrugbyfootballclub/news/memorable-international-debut-763865.html Whitchurch Rugby Club] Nick Kennard 5 Nov 2003 "Memorable International Debut For Thomas Davies"</ref> and Yorkshire Under 20s.<ref name=DoncasterFree>[http://www.doncasterfreepress.co.uk/sport/tom-is-first-home-grown-barbarian-1-503271 Donaster Free Press 16 Nov 2006] TOM IS FIRST HOME GROWN BARBARIAN</ref>  He has also played for the [[Barbarian F.C.|Barbarians]].<ref name=DoncasterFree/><ref name=TheStar2008>[http://www.thestar.co.uk/sport/football/rotherham/barbarians_call_up_for_knights_ace_1_235903  The Star (Sheffield) 28 February 2008] Barbarians call up for Knights ace</ref>

He retired from professional rugby in 2015 for a career in [[chemical engineering]], following a degree in that subject at the [[University of Sheffield]].<ref name=drfc/>
