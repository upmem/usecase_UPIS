'''Okopek''' {{IPAc-pl|o|'|k|o|p|e|k}} ({{lang-de |Wilhelmsberg}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) &ndash; TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|14|km|mi|0}} west of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|35|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|59|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].
