'''Icebird''' is an [[United States|American]] [[indie rock]] group from [[Los Angeles]]. Their sound is a mix of rock, post-punk, southern rock and metal.

==History==
The band originated in L.A.'s [[Silver Lake, Los Angeles, California|Silver Lake]] music scene. Icebird was started by two friends, Barry Monahan and Kate Wise who lived in [[Los Angeles]]. Monahan and his brother Mike, who soon joined the band, were born and raised on [[Long Island]], while Wise was from [[Huntsville, Alabama]]. Both of them were inspired and encouraged by the music of Kate’s brother’s band [[French Kicks]] in New York. They soon settled on a name the name Icebird after a coffee shop in the Koreatown neighborhood where both Monahan's lived.
