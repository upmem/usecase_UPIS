'''Sątopy''' {{IPAc-pl|s|o|n|'|t|o|p|y}} (German ''Santoppen'') is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Bisztynek]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|8|km|mi|0}} east of [[Bisztynek]], {{convert|26|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-east of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|46|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].

[[First Partition of Poland|Before 1772]] the area was part of [[Crown of the Kingdom of Poland|Kingdom of Poland]], 1772-1945 Prussia and Germany ([[East Prussia]]).

The village has a population of 210.
