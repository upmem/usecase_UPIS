'''Caleb Duvernay''' (born July 24, 1996) is an American professional [[association football|soccer]] player who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]].
==Career==
===College & Youth===
Duvernay played four years of [[college soccer]] at the [[North Carolina State University]] between 2014 and 2017.<ref>https://gopack.com/roster.aspx?rp_id=4805</ref>

While at college, Duvernay played with [[North Carolina FC U23|Carolina RailHawks U23s]] in the [[National Premier Soccer League]] in 2015 and 2016, and [[North Carolina FC U23]] in the [[USL PDL]] in 2017.<ref>https://www.uslleaguetwo.com/roster_players/18184412</ref>

===Professional===
On January 21, 2018, Duvernay was selected 61st overall in the [[2018 MLS SuperDraft]] by [[Portland Timbers]].<ref>https://www.timbers.com/post/2018/01/21/2018-mls-superdraft-timbers-select-four-players-final-two-rounds</ref> However, he did not sign with the club. He instead spent 2018 playing in the USL PDL with North Carolina U23.<ref>https://www.uslleaguetwo.com/roster_players/24811649</ref>

On March 15, 2019, Duvernay signed his first professional contract with [[USL Championship]] side [[North Carolina FC]].<ref>https://www.northcarolinafc.com/news_article/show/1004531</ref> He appeared in 16 games with the club over parts of two seasons before leaving the team on August 24, 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.northcarolinafc.com/news_article/show/1119195|title=NCFC and Caleb Duvernay Mutually Part Ways|website=NorthCarolinaFC.com|date=August 24, 2020|access-date=August 28, 2020}}</ref>
