'''''Wilful Youth''''' is a 1927 American [[silent film|silent]] [[drama film]] directed by [[Dallas M. Fitzgerald]] and starring [[Edna Murphy]], [[Kenneth Harlan]] and [[Jack Richardson (actor)|Jack Richardson]].<ref>Munden p.906</ref>

==Cast==
* [[Edna Murphy]] as Edna Tavernay 
* [[Kenneth Harlan]] as Jack Compton 
* [[Jack Richardson (actor)|Jack Richardson]] as Edward Compton 
* [[Walter Perry (actor)|Walter Perry]] as Terrance Clang 
* [[Jimmy Aubrey]] as Steve Daley 
* [[James Florey]] as Bull Thompson 
* [[Eugenie Forde]] as Mrs. Claudia Tavernay 
* [[Arthur Morrison (actor)|Arthur Morrison]] as Sheriff
