'''''Woman's Law''''' is a 1927 American [[silent film|silent]] [[drama film]] directed by [[Dallas M. Fitzgerald]] and starring [[Pat O'Malley (actor)|Pat O'Malley]], [[Lillian Rich]] and [[Audrey Ferris]].<ref>Munden p.922</ref>

==Cast==
* [[Pat O'Malley (actor)|Pat O'Malley]] as Trooper Bucky O'Hare 
* [[Lillian Rich]] as Helene 
* [[Ernest Wood (actor)|Ernest Wood]] as Vaughan Neil 
* [[John Cossar ]] as John Collon 
* [[Harold Miller (actor)|Harold Miller]] as Philip Harley 
* [[Edward Cecil (actor)|Edward Cecil]] as Inspetor Steele 
* [[Audrey Ferris]] as Rose La Pierre 
* [[Sam Allen (actor)|Sam Allen]] as Jules La Pierre
