'''Alex De Carolis''' (born 24 September 1992) is a Canadian professional [[Association football|soccer]] player who last played for [[HFX Wanderers FC|HFX Wanderers]] as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]].

==Club career==
===Early career===
De Carolis began playing soccer in [[Sarnia]] with local clubs Bluewater SC and Sarnia Fury.<ref name="TO">{{cite web |author1=Terry Bridge |title=Sarnia native Alex De Carolis signs pro contract with Sweden's Nora BK |url=https://www.theobserver.ca/2016/02/26/sarnia-native-alex-de-carolis-signs-pro-contract-with-swedens-nora-bk/wcm/413bd539-63f9-1f59-9042-a0026e1c2b0b |publisher=The Observer |accessdate=13 May 2019 |date=26 February 2016}}</ref> From 2012 to 2015, De Carolis attended [[Canisius College]] and in 2014 played for [[USL League Two|Premier Development League]] side [[FC London]].<ref name="TO" /> In 2015, he played for [[Seattle Sounders FC U-23]] and made the semi-finals of the [[2015 PDL season#PDL Championship|PDL Championship]].<ref name="TO" />

In February 2016, De Carolis signed with [[Division 3 (Swedish football)|Swedish Division 3]] side Nora BK.<ref name="TO" />

===Västerås SK===
On 7 March 2017, De Carolis signed with [[Division 1 (Swedish football)|Swedish Division 1]] side [[Västerås SK Fotboll|Västerås SK]].<ref>{{cite web |author1=Kevin Johansson |title=Klart – kanadensiske provspelaren skriver på för VSK: "Gillar hur supportrarna stöttar" |url=https://www.vlt.se/artikel/klart-kanadensiske-provspelaren-skriver-pa-for-vsk-gillar-hur-supportrarna-stottar |website=vlt.se |accessdate=13 May 2019 |language=Swedish |date=7 March 2017}}</ref> That season, he made fourteen league appearances for Västerås, scoring two goals.<ref name="SW">{{cite web |title=Alex De Carolis |url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/alex-de-carolis/477132/ |publisher=Soccerway |accessdate=13 May 2019}}</ref>

===Umeå FC===
On 24 January 2018, De Carolis signed with Swedish Division 1 side [[Umeå FC]].<ref>{{cite web |author1=David Pettersson |title=Umeå FC skriver tvåårskontrakt med provspelaren |url=https://www.vk.se/2212302/umea-fc-skriver-tvaarskontrakt-med-provspelaren |website=vk.se |accessdate=13 May 2019 |language=Swedish |date=24 January 2018}}</ref> He made 23 appearances for Umeå that season, including nine starts.<ref name="SW" />

===HFX Wanderers===
On 20 February 2019, De Carolis returned to Canada and signed with [[Canadian Premier League]] side [[HFX Wanderers FC|HFX Wanderers]].<ref>{{cite web |author1=Gareth Hampshire |title=Wanderers sign Halifax-born goalkeeper, versatile defender |url=https://hfxwanderersfc.canpl.ca/article/wanderers-sign-halifax-born-goalkeeper-versatile-defender |publisher=HFX Wanderers FC |accessdate=13 May 2019 |date=20 February 2019}}</ref> He made his debut on 11 May, in a 1–0 loss to [[Valour FC]]. In November 2020, De Carolis and the Wanderers agreed to mutually part ways.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canpl.ca/article/hfx-wanderers-fc-parts-ways-with-fullback-alex-de-carolis|date=November 19, 2020|title=HFX Wanderers FC parts ways with fullback Alex De Carolis}}</ref>
