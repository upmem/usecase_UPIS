'''Ora Marion Collard''' (September 17, 1902&ndash;April 11, 1961) was an American businessman and politician.

Collard was born in [[Norris City, Illinois]]. In 1904, Collard moved with his family to [[Herrin, Illinois]]. He went to the Herrin public schools. Collard owned the Collard Heating an Air Conditioning Company in Herrin, Illinois. He serve on the Herrin City Council from 1937 to 1941 and was a [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democrat]]. Collard also serve as an Illinois Deputy Fire Marshal and as the [[Williamson County, Illinois|Williamson County]] Deputy Sheriff. Collard served in the [[Illinois House of Representatives]] in 1949 and 1950.
