'''Marcellus Martin''' "'''Mike'''" '''McMichael''' (July 4, 1915 – November 13, 1997) was an American professional [[basketball]] player.<ref name=bbr>{{cite web| title =Marcellus McMichael NBL stats| work =basketball-reference.com| publisher =Sports Reference LLC| date = | url =https://www.basketball-reference.com/nbl/players/m/mcmicma01n.html | accessdate = May 13, 2019}}</ref> He played for the [[Akron Goodyear Wingfoots]] in the [[National Basketball League (United States)|National Basketball League]] during the 1938–39 season and averaged 3.9 points per game.<ref name=bbr/><ref name=peach>{{cite web| title =Mike McMichael | work =Peach Basket Society| publisher =| date = October 15, 2015 | url =http://peachbasketsociety.blogspot.com/2015/10/marcellus-mcmichael.html| accessdate = May 13, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| title = Marcellus McMichael Statistics| work =Just Sports Stats| publisher =| date = | url =https://www.justsportsstats.com/basketballstatsindex.php?player_id=mcmicmi01| accessdate = May 13, 2019}}</ref>
