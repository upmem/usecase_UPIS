'''Fanny Altenburger''' (born in 2000) is an Austrian actress.

== Life and career ==
The daughter of the actress [[Julia Stemberger]] and the violinist [[Christian Altenburger]],<ref name="kurier108.337.145">[https://kurier.at/kultur/festspiele-reichenau-eine-starparade-an-der-rax/108.337.145 ''Eine Starparade an der Rax'']. [[Der Kurier]], 16 January 2017, retrieved on 13 May 2019.</ref> she gained her first film experience in 2010 with ''[[Jud Süß - Film ohne Gewissen]]'' at the side of [[Tobias Moretti]] and [[Martina Gedeck]], who played her parents. Since 2011 Altenburger has regularly participated in the {{Ill|Festspiele Reichenau|de}} and appeared there in 2011 in the silent role of the page in ''[[Der Rosenkavalier]]'' by Richard Strauss, in 2012 in the stage version of ''[[Anna Karenina]]'' as Serjoscha, in 2013 in ''[[The Pillars of Society|Die Stützen der Gesellschaft]]'' by [[Henrik Ibsen]], 2015 as Frida Foldal in ''[[John Gabriel Borkman]]'' by Henrik Ibsen and as Köhlerkind in ''{{Ill|Der Alpenkönig und der Menschenfeind|de|3=Der Alpenkönig und der Menschenfeind|lt=The Alpine King and the Enemy of Man}}'', in 2016 as Fella Storch in ''[[Die Dämonen (Doderer)|Die Dämonen]]'' by [[Heimito von Doderer]] and in 2017 in Schnitzler's ''Im Spiel der Sommerlüfte'' on stage.<ref name="kurier108.337.145"/><ref>[http://derstandard.at/2000018729219/Borkman-Ein-Bankier-wollte-Menschenglueck-erschaffen ''"Borkman": Ein Bankier wollte Menschenglück erschaffen'']. [[Der Standard]], 7 July 2015, retrieved on  April 2017.</ref><ref>[http://diepresse.com/home/kultur/news/5245805/Festspiele-Reichenau_Schnitzler-im-gruenen-Irrgarten ''Festspiele Reichenau: Schnitzler im grünen Irrgarten''] Die Presse, 3 July 2017, retrieved on 13 May 2019.</ref>

In 2016, she appeared in the film ''[[Wir töten Stella (film)|Wir töten Stella]]'' by [[Julian Pölsler]]. In 2018, she completed school with the [[Matura]]. She appeared as Anna in ''{{Ill|Die letzte Party deines Lebens|de}}''.<ref>[https://www.heute.at/people/promis/story/Die-Tochter-von-Julia-Stemberger-startet-durch-51434337 ''Die Tochter von Julia Stemberger startet durch''], ''[[Heute]]'' dated 13 March 2018</ref> In the television series ''[[Counterpart (TV series)|Counterpart]]'', she played the role of Ethel in 2018.<ref>[https://showsnob.com/2019/02/17/counterpart-series-finale-recap/ ''Counterpart series finale recap: Better Angels''], 17 February 2019</ref> She performed in the play ''Honig im Kopf'' by {{Ill|Florian Battermann|de}} based on the [[Honig im Kopf|eponymous film]] by [[Til Schweiger]].
