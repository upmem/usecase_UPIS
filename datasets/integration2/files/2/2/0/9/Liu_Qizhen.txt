'''Liu Qizhen''' ({{lang-zh|刘启祯}}; born 17 September 1995) is a [[China|Chinese]] athlete specialising in the [[javelin throw]].<ref>{{iaaf name|311471}}</ref> He won a silver medal at the [[2018 Asian Games]].

His personal best in the event is 82.22 metres set in Jakarta in 2018.
