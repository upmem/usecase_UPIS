'''Kumkiejmy''' {{IPAc-pl|k|u|m|'|k|J|e|I|m|y}} ({{lang-de|Kumkeim}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|10|km|mi|0}} north of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|22|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|63|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany ([[East Prussia]]).
