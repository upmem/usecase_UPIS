'''Mark van Heerden''' is one of the two guitarists for South African rock band [[Wickhead]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.citizen.co.za/index/article.aspx?pDesc=21066,1,22|title=Wordly{{sic|hide=y}} Wickhead|last=Pearman-Owens|first=Cathryn|date=2 August 2006|work=[[The Citizen (South Africa)|The Citizen]]|accessdate=29 May 2010}} {{dead link|date=July 2018}}</ref> He is known for his wild stage personality and his way of saying the wrong thing in interviews.

Mark was born, and grew up, in [[Pietermaritzburg]], [[South Africa]]. He now lives in [[California]].
