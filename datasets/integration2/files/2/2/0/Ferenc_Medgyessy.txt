'''Ferenc Medgyessy''' (1881 in [[Debrecen]], [[Kingdom of Hungary|Hungary]] – 1958 in [[Budapest]], Hungary) was a [[List of Hungarian sculptors|Hungarian sculptor]] and physician. After graduating in medicine he studied art in [[Paris]], later he studied [[Michelangelo]] and the [[Etruscan civilization|Etruscan]] art in [[Florence]]. His art was dominated by folk [[Realism (visual arts)|realism]].
