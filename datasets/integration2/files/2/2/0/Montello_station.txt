'''Montello''' is an [[MBTA Commuter Rail]] [[train station|station]] in [[Brockton, Massachusetts]]. It serves the [[Middleborough/Lakeville Line]]. Located north of downtown Brockton, it has two full-length high-level platforms serving the line's two tracks, and is fully [[MBTA accessibility|accessible]].

==History==
[[File:A gazetteer of the state of Massachusetts - with numerous illustrations (1890) (14766729382).jpg|thumb|left|1880s woodcutting of the former Montello station]]
The station opened on September 29, 1997 along with the rest of the [[Old Colony Lines (MBTA)|Old Colony Lines]] service.<ref name=netransit /> A [[New Haven Railroad]] station was located at Montello on the [[Fall River Railroad (1846)|Middleborough Main Line]] until the Old Colony Division closed in 1959.
