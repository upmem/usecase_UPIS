'''Seán Buckley''' (born 1938 in [[Freshford, County Kilkenny]]) is an [[Irish people|Irish]] former sportsperson.  He played [[hurling]] with his local club [[St Lachtain's GAA|St Lachtain's]] and was a member of the [[Kilkenny GAA|Kilkenny]] senior inter-county team from 1957 until 1970.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hoganstand.com/Kilkenny/Profile.aspx|title=Kilkenny GAA profile|publisher=www.hoganstand.com|accessdate=2010-08-01|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111014192436/http://hoganstand.com/Kilkenny/Profile.aspx|archivedate=2011-10-14}}</ref>
