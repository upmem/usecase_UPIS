'''Brad Yoder''' is a songwriter/performer who resides in [[Pittsburgh]]. He is well known in the Pittsburgh music scene, and his music has appeared in television shows such as ''[[NUMB3RS]]'' and ''[[Dawson's Creek]]''.<ref>[http://www.performingartsschools.com/interviews/brad-yoder.html Interview with Brad Yoder]</ref> He was voted "Best Acoustic Artist" by readers of the ''[[Pittsburgh City Paper]]'' in 2003, 2004, 2006 and 2007.<ref>[http://www.pittsburghcitypaper.ws/gyrobase/Content?oid=oid%3A26247 ''Pittsburgh City Paper'' Best of 2003]</ref><ref>[http://www.pittsburghcitypaper.ws/gyrobase/Content?oid=oid%3A27601 ''Pittsburgh City Paper'' Best of 2004]</ref><ref>[http://www.pittsburghcitypaper.ws/gyrobase/Content?oid=oid%3A20397 ''Pittsburgh City Paper Best'' of 2006]</ref>
