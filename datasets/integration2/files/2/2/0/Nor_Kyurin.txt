'''Nor Kyurin''' ({{lang-hy|Նոր Կյուրին}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Nor Kurin''') is a village in the [[Ararat (province)|Ararat Province]] of [[Armenia]].  Nor Kyurin means New Kyurin, pronounced [gjurin] or [gyrin] in [[Western Armenian]].  It was named after the city of [[Gürün]], which is known as Gurin or Gyurin to Armenians ([[Traditional Armenian orthography|TAO]]: Կիւրին, [[Reformed Armenian Orthography|RAO]]: Կյուրին), was a part of historical [[Lesser Armenia|Pokr Hayk]] and had a significant Armenian population until the [[Armenian genocide]].
