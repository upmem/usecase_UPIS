'''Dęby''' {{IPAc-pl|'|d|e|m|b|y}} ({{lang-de|Eichen}})<ref name="Former Territory of Germany">{{cite web |url=http://gov.genealogy.net/item/show/EICHENKO04FH |title=Former Territory of Germany|date=2017-11-08 |language=German}}</ref> is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|7|km|mi|0}} north of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|23|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|61|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].
