'''M. J. Ward Feed Mill Complex''' is a national [[Historic district (United States)|historic district]] located at [[Bath (village), New York|Bath]] in [[Steuben County, New York]]. The district is a single property containing a three-story feed mill and store (1909) with attached grain handling and storage facilities (1976), a storage barn (ca 1870), and a residence (ca. 1870).<ref name="nrhpinv_ny">{{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=8538|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: M. J. Ward Feed Mill Complex|date=n.d.|accessdate=2009-10-26 |author=Kathleen LaFrank|publisher=[[New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation]]}} ''See also:'' {{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=8542|title=Accompanying five photos}}</ref>

It was listed on the [[National Register of Historic Places]] in 1991.<ref name="nris"/>
