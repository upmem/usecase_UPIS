'''''Love Is Only a Word''''' ({{lang-de|'''Liebe ist nur ein Wort'''}}) is a 1971 West German [[drama film]] directed by [[Alfred Vohrer]] and starring [[Judy Winter]], [[Herbert Fleischmann]] and [[Malte Thorsten]].<ref>Bock & Bergfelder p.504</ref>

* [[Franz Rudnick]] as Kasilowski
* [[Frank Süssenguth]] as Pilot Teddy Behnke
