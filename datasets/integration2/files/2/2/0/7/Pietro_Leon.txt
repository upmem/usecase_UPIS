{{Use dmy dates|date=February 2020}}'''Pietro Leon''' (died 1474) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as [[Bishop of Ceneda]] (1445–1474) and [[Bishop of Ossero]] (1436–1445).<ref name=HierarchiaII>{{cite book|url=https://archive.org/details/hierarchiacathol02eubeuoft|first=Konrad|last=Eubel|title=HIERARCHIA CATHOLICA MEDII ET RECENTIORIS AEVI Vol II|pages=[https://archive.org/details/hierarchiacathol02eubeuoft/page/77 77] and 124|date=1914|publisher=Libreria Regensbergiana|location=Münster|edition=second|ISBN=}} ''(in Latin)''</ref><ref name=CathHierPieLeon>[http://www.catholic-hierarchy.org/bishop/bleonpi.html "Bishop Pietro Leon"] ''[[Catholic-Hierarchy.org]]''. David M. Cheney. Retrieved January 4, 2017</ref><ref name=CathHierOssero>[http://www.catholic-hierarchy.org/diocese/do506.html "Diocese of Ossero (Osor)"] ''[[Catholic-Hierarchy.org]]''. David M. Cheney. Retrieved June 16, 2016</ref><ref name=GCathOsor>[http://www.gcatholic.org/dioceses/former/t1330.htm "Titular Episcopal See of Osor"] ''GCatholic.org''. Gabriel Chow. Retrieved June 16, 2016</ref> 

==Biography==
On 6 Feb 1436, Pietro Leon was appointed during the papacy of [[Pope Eugene IV]] as [[Bishop of Ossero]].<ref name=HierarchiaII /><ref name=CathHierPieLeon />
On 4 Jun 1445, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Eugene IV as [[Bishop of Ceneda]].<ref name=HierarchiaII /><ref name=CathHierPieLeon />
He served as Bishop of Ceneda until his death in 1474.<ref name=CathHierPieLeon  />
