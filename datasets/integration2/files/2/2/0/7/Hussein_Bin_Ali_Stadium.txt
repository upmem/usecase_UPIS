'''Hussein Bin Ali Stadium''' is an [[association football]] stadium in [[Hebron]].

==West Bank Premier League==
The stadium is home to [[Ahli Al-Khaleel]], [[Shabab Al-Khalil SC]] and [[Shabab Alsamu]] in the [[West Bank Premier League]]. Playing for Shabab, [[Palestine national football team]] player [[Ashraf Nu'man]] scored two goals at the venue against [[Thaqafi Tulkarem]] in the 2016-17 season.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Levy|first1=Uri|title=Good news and tragedy in Mideast football this week|url=https://www.alaraby.co.uk/english/blog/2016/12/11/good-news-and-tragedy-in-mideast-football-this-week|website=The New Arab|accessdate=26 June 2017}}</ref>

==Palestine Cup==
The 2015 [[Palestine Cup]] final between Ahli Al-Khaleel and [[Ittihad Shujaiyah]] took place at the stadium - the first final to be contested by the top teams from the [[West Bank Premier League]] and [[Gaza Strip League]] in 15 years.<ref name=cupfinal /> The intervention of [[FIFA]], [[UEFA]] and the [[Asian Football Confederation|AFC]] was necessary to secure permission from Israeli authorities for players to travel from the [[Gaza Strip]] to the [[West Bank]].<ref name="Final">{{cite web|title=Ahli Al-Khaleel lift Palestine Cup in dramatic fashion|url=http://www.footballpalestine.com/2015/08/ahli-al-khaleel-lift-palestine-cup-in.html|website=Football Palestine|accessdate=26 June 2017}}</ref> The match was attended by President of the [[Palestinian Football Association]] [[Jibril Rajoub]], MK [[Ahmad Tibi]] and the mayors of [[Bethlehem]] and [[Hebron]],<ref name=cupfinal /> and sponsored by the [[Bank of Palestine]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Bank of Palestine, the primary sponsor of the final game in Palestine Cup, congratulates Ahli Club for winning the Cup|url=https://www.bankofpalestine.com/en/media-center/newsroom/details/498|website=Bank of Palestine|accessdate=26 June 2017}}</ref>

The stadium was again the venue for the 2016 cup final, won by Ahli Al-Khaleel against [[Shabab Khan Yunis]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Levy|first1=Uri|title=Hebron's Ahli defeat Khan Younis to win Palestine Cup|url=https://www.alaraby.co.uk/english/society/2016/8/2/hebrons-ahli-defeat-khan-younis-to-win-palestine-cup|website=The New Arab|accessdate=26 June 2017}}</ref>
