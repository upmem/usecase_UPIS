The '''Unidad Deportiva Francisco Gómez Palacio''' is a multi-use [[stadium]] in [[Gómez Palacio, Durango|Gómez Palacio]], [[Durango]], [[Mexico]].  It is currently used mostly for [[football (soccer)|football]] matches and is the home stadium for [[Constructores de Gómez Palacio|Constructores]].  The stadium has a capacity of 4,000 people.
