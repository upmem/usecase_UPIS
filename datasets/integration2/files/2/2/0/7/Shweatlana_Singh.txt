'''Shweatlana Singh''' is a basketball player and reportedly is the tallest woman in [[Meerut]], [[India]] at {{convert|6|ft|8|in|cm|abbr=on}} in 2007. She is probably most notable for giving birth to Karan Singh, who according to [[Guinness World Records]] was the heaviest toddler in 2008 and who was claimed to be the tallest eight-year-old boy at {{convert|6|ft|6|in|cm|abbr=on}} (only {{convert|2|in|cm|sigfig=1}} shorter than [[LeBron James]]) in 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.dailystar.co.uk/news/world-news/622525/Worlds-tallest-boy-Guinness-Book-of-Records-Meerut-India-Karan-Singh|title=Worlds Tallest Boy}}</ref> In 2014, Karan Singh was already {{convert|5|ft|7|in|cm|abbr=on}} at five years old.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2014/09/19/meet-a-5-year-old-who-is-nearly-6-feet-tall/|title=Meet a five year old who is nearly six feet tall}}</ref> Svetlana Singh (India) claims to be {{convert|7|ft|2|in|cm|abbr=on}}, but was actually measured at {{convert|6|ft|8|in|abbr=on}} tall.{{cn|date=January 2018}} Her son was claimed to be {{convert|4|ft|5|in|cm|abbr=on}} tall at the age of 2.5 years old and was reported to be the world's tallest toddler.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thetallestman.com/svetlanasingh.htm|title=Svetlana Singh 6 ft 8 inches}}</ref>
