'''''All People Will Be Brothers''''' ({{lang-de|'''Alle Menschen werden Brüder'''}}) is a 1973 West German [[drama film]] directed by [[Alfred Vohrer]] and starring [[Harald Leipnitz]], [[Doris Kunstmann]] and [[Rainer von Artenfels]].<ref>Bock & Bergfelder p.504</ref>
