The '''Bruce Lee statue in Hong Kong''' is a memorial figure of deceased [[martial artist]], [[Bruce Lee]].<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/entertainment/4711947.stm BBC NEWS | Entertainment | Hong Kong's honor for Bruce Lee<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> The [[Hong Kong]] memorial was built on behalf of Bruce Lee, who died on 20 July 1973 at the age of 32.

==History==
The Hong Kong Bruce Lee Club raised $[[United States dollar|US 100,000]] for a statue to be erected after pleas to the government to honour his legacy.<ref>[http://www.theage.com.au/news/people/hong-kong-unveils-bruce-lee-statue/2005/11/27/1133026345589.html Hong Kong unveils Bruce Lee statue - People - Entertainment - theage.com.au<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> A 2.5 metre [[Bronze sculpture|bronze statue]] by artist [[Cao Chong-en]] was erected along the [[Avenue of Stars, Hong Kong|Avenue of Stars]] attraction near the waterfront at [[Tsim Sha Tsui]].<ref>[http://cbs5.com/entertainment/Bruce.Lee.Hong.2.257816.html cbs5.com - Bruce Lee Fans Aim for Hong Kong Statue<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080106153737/http://cbs5.com/entertainment/Bruce.Lee.Hong.2.257816.html |date=January 6, 2008 }}</ref> It shows a classic 'ready to strike' Bruce Lee pose, as seen in the 1972 movie ''[[Fist of Fury]]''. Hew Kuan-yau, a member of the Bruce Lee Fan Club Committee, said, "We want the people to know about the legend of Bruce Lee." The statue was unveiled by Bruce Lee's brother [[Robert Lee (musician)|Robert Lee]] on 27 November 2005, celebrating what would have been Bruce's 65th birthday. 

The statue was featured on the 10th leg of the American reality TV show ''[[The Amazing Race 17]]'',<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ledger-enquirer.com/news/local/article29151235.html|title=Amazing Race 2010: Who got eliminated in the Amazing Race Season 17 Episode 10?|work=[[Ledger-Enquirer]]|last=Riquelmy|first=Alan|date=November 28, 2010|accessdate=April 1, 2020}}</ref> the 10th leg of ''[[The Amazing Race Norge 1]]'',<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.newth.net/eirik/2012/05/31/the-amazing-race-norge-sesong-1-the-good-the-bad-and-the-ugly/|title=The Amazing Race Norge sesong 1: The Good, the Bad and the Ugly|language=Norwegian|trans-title=The Amazing Race Norge season 1: The Good, the Bad and the Ugly|website=Erik Newth|accessdate=April 1, 2020}}</ref> and the 3rd leg of ''[[The Amazing Race Canada 2]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.huffingtonpost.ca/2014/07/23/amazing-race-canada-season-2-episode-3-recap_n_5613080.html|title='Amazing Race Canada' Season 2, Episode 3 Recap: To Hong Kong|work=[[HuffPost]]|last=Adams|first=Joanna|date=July 23, 2014|accessdate=April 1, 2020}}</ref>
