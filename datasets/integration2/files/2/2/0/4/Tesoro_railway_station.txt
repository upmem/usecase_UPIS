'''Tesoro''' ({{lang-it|Stazione di Tesoro}}) is a railway station in the [[Italy|Italian]] city of [[Bari]], in the [[Province of Bari]], [[Apulia]]. The station lies on the [[Bari–San Paolo railway]]. The train services are operated by [[Ferrotramviaria]].

==Train services==
The station is served by the following service(s):<ref>[http://www.ferrovienordbarese.it/tmp/downloads/san_paolo_inv_2014.pdf Ferrovie del Nord Barese timetable]{{full citation needed|date=February 2015}}</ref>

*Bari Metropolitan services (''FM1'') Ospedale - Bari
