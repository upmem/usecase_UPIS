The '''Ministry of Commerce and Industry''' is a [[Ministry (government department)|ministry]] of the [[Government of Haiti]]. This ministry is responsible for [[commerce]] and [[Industrial sector|industry]] throughout the country, along with playing an integral role in the [[Government of Haiti#Cabinet|Prime Minister's Cabinet]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Haiti - Economy : The Ministry of Commerce supports entrepreneurship in Haiti - HaitiLibre.com : Haiti news 7/7|url=http://www.haitilibre.com/en/news-9956-haiti-economy-the-ministry-of-commerce-supports-entrepreneurship-in-haiti.html|website=HaitiLibre.com|accessdate=22 December 2016}}</ref>
