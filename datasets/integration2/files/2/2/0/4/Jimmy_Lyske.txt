'''James Herbert Alexander Lyske''' (7 October 1932&nbsp;– 1974) was a Northern Irish [[association football|footballer]] who played as a [[full back (association football)|full back]] in the [[Irish Football League]] for [[Glenavon F.C.|Glenavon]], in the [[the Football League|English Football League]] for [[Darlington F.C.|Darlington]],<ref name=Hugman>{{cite book |editor-first=Barry J. |editor-last=Hugman |title=The PFA Premier & Football League Players' Records 1946–1998 |date=1998 |publisher=Queen Anne Press |isbn=978-1-85291-585-8 |page=339}}</ref> and for [[Southern Football League|Southern League]] club [[Yeovil Town F.C.|Yeovil Town]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ciderspace.co.uk/asp/history/yeovil-town-story23.asp |title=The Yeovil Town Story: Part 23: Season 1958–59: The Southern League expands |website=Ciderspace |date=27 July 2009 |accessdate=16 October 2014}}</ref> He was on the books of [[Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland]] without playing for them in the League.<ref name=Hugman/>

Lyske played four times for Ireland Schools in 1947<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.britishnewspaperarchive.co.uk/viewer/bl/0000564/19470605/096/0005 |title=Dens Park will show who's top of the class |newspaper=The Courier |location=Dundee |date=5 June 1947 |page=5 |url-access=subscription |via=[[British Newspaper Archive]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://myweb.tiscali.co.uk/nisfa/U15%20Caps.htm |title=NISFA U15 & U16 international players |work=Northern Ireland Schools' Football Association |accessdate=16 October 2014}}</ref> and represented [[Northern Ireland national under-19 football team|Northern Ireland youth]] in 1951.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://nifootball.blogspot.co.uk/2012/01/northern-ireland-youth-match-details.html |title=Northern Ireland Youth match details 1948–1980 |work=Northern Ireland's Footballing Greats |editor=Dewart, Jonny |date=2 January 2012 |accessdate=16 October 2014}}</ref>
