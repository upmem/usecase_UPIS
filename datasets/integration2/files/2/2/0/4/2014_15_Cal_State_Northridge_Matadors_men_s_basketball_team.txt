The '''2014–15 Cal State Northridge Matadors men's basketball team''' represented [[California State University, Northridge]] during the [[2014–15 NCAA Division I men's basketball season]]. The Matadors, led by second year head coach [[Reggie Theus]], played their home games at the [[Matadome]] as members of the [[Big West Conference]]. They finished the season 9–24, 4–12 in Big West play to finish in eighth place. They lost in the first round of the [[2015 Big West Conference Men's Basketball Tournament|Big West Tournament]] to UC Davis.
