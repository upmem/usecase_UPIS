'''''The Dead Don't Dream''''' is a 1948 American [[Western (genre)|Western]] film directed by [[George Archainbaud]] and written by Francis Rosenwald. The picture stars [[William Boyd (actor)|William Boyd]], [[Andy Clyde]], [[Rand Brooks]], Mary Ware, John Parrish and [[Leonard Penn]], and was released in 1948 by [[United Artists]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/72484/The-Dead-Don-t-Dream/|title=The Dead Don't Dream (1948) - Overview - TCM.com|work=Turner Classic Movies|accessdate=16 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://movies.tvguide.com/the-dead-dont-dream/112454|title=The Dead Don't Dream|work=TV Guide|accessdate=16 October 2014}}</ref>
