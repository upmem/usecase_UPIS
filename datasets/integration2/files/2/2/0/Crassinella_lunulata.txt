'''''Crassinella lunulata''''', or the '''lunate crassinella''', is a species of very small [[bivalve]] [[mollusc]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Crassatellidae]]. It can be found along the Atlantic coast of North America, ranging from [[Massachusetts]] to [[Texas]].<ref>Abbott, R.T. & Morris, P.A. ''A Field Guide to Shells: Atlantic and Gulf Coasts and the West Indies.'' New York: Houghton Mifflin, 1995. 43.</ref>
