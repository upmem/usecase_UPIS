'''Pareżki''' {{IPAc-pl|p|a|'|r|e|sz|k|i}} ({{lang-de |Parösken}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) &ndash; TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|8|km|mi|0}} north of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|26|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|62|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany ([[East Prussia]]).
