'''Nerkin Giratagh''' ({{lang-hy|Ներքին Գիրաթաղ}}), is an abandoned village in the [[Kajaran Municipality]] of [[Syunik Province]], [[Armenia]]. 


== See also ==
* [[Lernadzor]]

==History==
Nerkin Giratagh was formerly part of the [[Community (Armenia)|rural community]] of [[Verin Giratagh]], which was merged into the community of Lernadzor.<ref>{{ARMSTATdata|44}}</ref>  The [[National Statistical Service of the Republic of Armenia]] (ARMSTAT) reported that Nerqin Giratagh was uninhabited at the 2001 and 2011 censuses.<ref>{{ArmenianCensus|210}}</ref>
