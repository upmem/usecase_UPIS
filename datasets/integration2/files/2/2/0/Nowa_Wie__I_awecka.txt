'''Nowa Wieś Iławecka''' {{IPAc-pl|'|n|o|w|a|-|'|w|j|e|ś|-|i|ł|a|'|w|e|c|k|a}} ({{lang-de|Neuendorf}})<ref name="Former Territory of Germany">{{cite web|url=http://gov.genealogy.net/item/show/NEUORFKO04HH|title=Former Territory of Germany|date=2017-11-08|language=German}}</ref> is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górowo Iławeckie]], within [[Bartoszyce County]], [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]], in northern Poland, close to the border with the [[Kaliningrad Oblast]] of [[Russia]].<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) &ndash; TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> 

It lies approximately {{convert|12|km|mi|0}} north-east of [[Górowo Iławeckie]], {{convert|16|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of [[Bartoszyce]], and {{convert|62|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of the regional capital [[Olsztyn]].
