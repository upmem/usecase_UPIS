'''Robbie Tucker''' (born April 5, 2001)<ref>{{Cite tweet |number=849695109893378049 |user=RobbieTucker_ |title=So I guess I'm 16 now |date=5 April 2017}}</ref> is an [[United States|American]] [[actor]]. His best known role to date is that of [[Fenmore Baldwin]] on the [[CBS]] [[soap opera]] ''[[The Young and the Restless]]''. Tucker has also starred on other series, such as ''[[Criminal Minds]]'', ''[[FlashForward]]'' and ''[[It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia]]''. He has also appeared in the films ''[[Prom (film)|Prom]]'' and ''[[Little Fockers]]''.

In 2012, Tucker was nominated at the [[33rd Young Artist Awards]] for his performance in ''[[Prom (film)|Prom]]'' and won for his role in ''[[The Young and the Restless]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youngartistawards.org/noms33.html|title=33rd Annual Young Artist Awards|accessdate=May 7, 2012|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120404162133/http://www.youngartistawards.org/noms33.html|archivedate=April 4, 2012|df=}}</ref>

Brother of actress [[Jillian Rose Reed]].
