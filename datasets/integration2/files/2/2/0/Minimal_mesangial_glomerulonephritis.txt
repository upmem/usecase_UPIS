'''Minimal mesangial glomerulonephritis''' is a type of [[glomerulonephritis]] is seen in 10% to 25% of [[Systemic lupus erythematosus|SLE]] cases, and is associated with mild clinical [[symptoms]]. [[Immune complexes]] deposit in the [[mesangium]], with a slight increase in the [[mesangial matrix]] and [[cellularity]].<ref name="isbn0-8089-2366-8">{{cite book |author1=Robbins, Stanley L. |author2=Kumar, Vinay |title=Robbins basic pathology |edition= |language= |publisher=Saunders/Elsevier |location= |year=2007 |origyear= |pages=142 |quote= |isbn=978-0-8089-2366-4 |oclc= |doi= |url= |accessdate=}}</ref>
