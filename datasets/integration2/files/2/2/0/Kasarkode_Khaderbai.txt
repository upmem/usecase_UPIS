'''''Kasaragod Khader Bhai''''' is a [[List of Malayalam films of 1992|1992]] Indian [[Malayalam cinema|Malayalam]]-language [[comedy film]] directed by [[Thulasidas]] and starring [[Jagadish]], [[Siddique (actor)|Siddique]], [[Ashokan (actor)|Ashokan]], [[Zainuddin (actor)|Zainuddin]], [[Innocent (actor)|Innocent]], [[Mala Aravindan]], [[Alummoodan]], [[Babu Antony]], [[Sunitha (actress)|Sunitha]], [[Suchitra (actress)|Suchitra]] and [[Philomina]]. The film is a sequel to 1991 film ''[[Mimics Parade]]''. In 2010, it spawned a sequel titled ''[[Again Kasargod Khader Bhai]]''.

==Plot==
The film is about how Kasargod Khader Bhai ([[Alummoodan]]) and his son Kasim Bhai ([[Babu Antony]]) tries to take revenge on the mimicry artists who send Khader Bhai to jail (This is shown in the prequel).
