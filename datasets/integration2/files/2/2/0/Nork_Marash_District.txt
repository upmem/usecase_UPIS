'''Nork-Marash''' ({{lang-hy|Նորք-Մարաշ վարչական շրջան, Nork-Maraš varčakan šrĵan}}), is one of the [[Districts of Yerevan|12 districts]] of [[Yerevan]], the capital of [[Armenia]]. It is located to the east of the city centre.  It is bordered by the [[Kentron District]] form the west and the north, [[Nor Nork District|Nor Nork]] from the east and [[Erebuni District|Erebuni]] from the south. The name of the district is derived from the [[Nork, Yerevan|Nork]] neighbourhood of Yerevan and the ancient major of [[Kahramanmaraş|Marash]] in [[Turkey|Republic of Turkey]].<ref>[https://www.yerevan.am/am/districts/nork-marash/ Nork-Marash at Yerevan.am]</ref> The district is unofficially divided into smaller neighborhoods such as Nork and [[Nor Marash]].

The district was formed in 1996 through the merger of Nork and [[Nor Marash]] neighborhoods. It has an area of 4&nbsp;km² and a population of 12,049 (2011 census).
