'''Lai-ye Rudbar''' ({{lang-fa|لايي رودبار}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Lā’ī-ye Rūdbār''', '''Lā’ī Rūdbār''' and '''Lāy-e Rūdbār''')<ref>{{GEOnet3|48261|Lai-ye Rudbar}}</ref> is a village in [[Zarem Rud Rural District]], [[Hezarjarib District]], [[Neka County]], [[Mazandaran Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 339, in 97 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|02}}</ref>
