'''Ab Chin''' ({{lang-fa|اب چين}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Āb Chīn''') is a village in [[Zarem Rud Rural District]], [[Hezarjarib District]], [[Neka County]], [[Mazandaran Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 271, in 75 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|02}}</ref>
