'''Francisco "Francis" Javier Solar González''' (born 14 September 1983) is a Spanish [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Marbella FC]] as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]].

==Club career==
Born in [[Málaga]], [[Andalusia]], Francis finished his graduation with local [[Málaga CF]], and made his senior debuts with the [[Málaga CF B|B-team]] on 30 August 2003, in a 2–3 away loss against [[UD Almería]] for the [[Segunda División]] championship.<ref>[http://actas.rfef.es/actas/RFEF_CmpPartido?cod_primaria=1000144&CodActa=111 Almería 3–2 Málaga B]; [[Royal Spanish Football Federation|RFEF]], 30 August 2003 {{in lang|es}}</ref> He played in [[Segunda División B]] but also in [[Tercera División]] in the following years, representing [[Valencia CF Mestalla]], [[CD Eldense]], [[UD Juventud Barrio del Cristo]] (two stints), [[Ontinyent CF]] and [[CD Olímpic de Xàtiva]];<ref>[http://www.saforguia.com/deportes/tabid/359/titular/seligrat_ya_es_entrenador_del_cd_ol%C3%ADmpic/idnoticia/23697/default.aspx Seligrat ya es entrenador del CD Olímpic (Seligrat is already manager of CD Olímpic)] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131105202437/http://www.saforguia.com/deportes/tabid/359/titular/seligrat_ya_es_entrenador_del_cd_ol%C3%ADmpic/idnoticia/23697/default.aspx |date=2013-11-05 }}; Safor Guia, 14 June 2010 {{in lang|es}}</ref> with the latter he achieved promotion from the fourth level in [[2010–11 Tercera División|2011]], appearing in 27 matches.

On 22 June 2014, after four full seasons as an undisputed starter, Francis moved to fellow third division side [[CF Reus Deportiu]].<ref>[http://www.diaridetarragona.com/deportes/25449/el-cf-reus-ata-a-francis El CF Reus ata a Francis (CF Reus signs Francis)]; Diari de Tarragona, 22 June 2014 {{in lang|es}}</ref> On 16 January of the following year, after being a backup to [[Édgar Badía]], he joined [[Marbella FC]] in the same level.<ref>[http://www.marbella24horas.es/futbol/el-marbella-fc-ficha-el-portero-francis-solar-y-al-mediocentro-villar-9594 El Marbella FC ficha el portero Francis Solar y al mediocentro Villar (Marbella FC signs goalkeeper Francis Solar and central midfielder Villar)]; Marbella 24 Horas, 16 January 2015 {{in lang|es}}</ref>
