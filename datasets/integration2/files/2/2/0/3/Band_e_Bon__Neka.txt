'''Band-e Bon''' ({{lang-fa|بندبن}})<ref>{{GEOnet3|45614|Band-e Bon}}</ref> is a village in [[Zarem Rud Rural District]], [[Hezarjarib District]], [[Neka County]], [[Mazandaran Province]], [[Iran]].

It is located in the [[Alborz|Alborz (''Elburz'') mountain range]].

At the 2006 census, its population was 98, in 28 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|02}}</ref>
