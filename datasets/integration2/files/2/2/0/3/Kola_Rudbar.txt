'''Kola Rudbar''' ({{lang-fa|كلارودبار}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Kolā Rūdbār''' and '''Kalā Rūdbār''')<ref>{{GEOnet3|48255|Kola Rudbar}}</ref> is a village in [[Zarem Rud Rural District]], [[Hezarjarib District]], [[Neka County]], [[Mazandaran Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 229, in 56 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|02}}</ref>
