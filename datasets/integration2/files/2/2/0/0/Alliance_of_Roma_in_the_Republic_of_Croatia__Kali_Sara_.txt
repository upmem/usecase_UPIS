The '''Alliance of Roma in the Republic of Croatia "Kali Sara"''' ({{lang-hr|Savez Roma u Republici Hrvatskoj "Kali Sara"}}, {{lang-rom|Kali Sara}}) is an organization of the [[Romani people in Croatia]].<ref name="Ljudska prava i prava nacionalnih manjina">{{cite web|url=https://pravamanjina.gov.hr/nacionalne-manjine/nacionalne-manjine-u-republici-hrvatskoj/romi/371|title=Romi|publisher=[[Government of Croatia]] (Office for Human Rights and Rights of National Minorities) |format= |date= | accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> Its seat is located in Croatian capital city of [[Zagreb]].<ref name="Ljudska prava i prava nacionalnih manjina"/> The organization was established in July 2007 and its first president was [[Veljko Kajtazi]], future member of the [[Croatian Parliament]].<ref name="Udruga-O nama">{{cite web|url=http://kalisara.hr/o-nama/|title=RomiO SAVEZU ROMA REPUBLIKE HRVATSKE "KALI SARA" |publisher=Alliance of Roma in the Republic of Croatia "Kali Sara" |format= |date= | accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> On the occasion of publication of the first Romani-Croatian dictionary in 2008, the Alliance initiated the signing of the ''Charter of the Romani Language'' which was signed by 157 individuals.<ref name="Udruga-O nama"/> On the date of the publication of the dictionary on November 5 (authored by the Alliance's president), the local and international Roma community celebrated the ''World Day of Romani Language''.<ref name="Udruga-O nama"/> The Croatian Parliament formally recognized ''Romani Language Day'' on May 25, 2012.<ref name="Sabor-Romani Language Day">{{cite web|url=https://www.vecernji.hr/vijesti/sabor-podrzao-inicijativu-o-5-studenom-danu-romskog-jezika-413816|title=Sabor podržao inicijativu o 5. studenom, Danu romskog jezika|publisher=[[Večernji list]]| format=|date=25 May 2012| accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> In 2015 [[UNESCO]] accepted the alliance's initiative to recognize the [[Romani language]] as a part of global cultural heritage by recognizing the World Day of Romani Language.<ref name="Srbi.hr">{{cite web|url=https://srbi.hr/veljko-kajtazi-srbe-i-rome-u-hrvatskoj-veze-puno-toga/|title=Veljko Kajtazi: „Srbe i Rome u Hrvatskoj veže puno toga“|publisher=Srbi.hr| format=|date=23 April 2020| accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> The formal proposal to UNESCO was made by the Republic of Croatia.<ref name="Anadolija">{{cite web|url=https://www.aa.com.tr/ba/balkan/hrvatska-deseto-obilje%C5%BEavanje-svjetskog-dana-romskog-jezika/1635199|title=Hrvatska: Deseto obilježavanje Svjetskog dana romskog jezika|publisher=[[Anadolu Agency]]| format=|date=4 November 2019| accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> In 2018, the Alliance published the monograph "''Kali Sara-The First Ten Years''".<ref name="HRT-10 Godina">{{cite web|url=https://vijesti.hrt.hr/443672/predstavljena-publikacija-kali-sara-prvih-deset-godina|title=Predstavljena publikacija 'Kali sara - prvih deset godina'|publisher=[[Hrvatska radiotelevizija]]| format=|date=14 May 2018| accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref>
