'''Angelique Monet'''<ref>{{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1787877/|title=IMDB Filmography credits producer, writer and director  |publisher=[[IMDB|Internet Movie Database]]|accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> is the former Ms. Black South Carolina USA<ref>{{{cite web|url=https://www.goupstate.com/article/NC/19970101/News/605180104/SJ|title=Miss Black S.C. sets sights on another crown; Smith planning to compete in Miss Black USA pageant  |publisher= [[Gannett |USA TODAY NETWORK]]|accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref>
at the start of the 2000s she first performed as a [[Ventriloquism|ventriloquist]]<ref>{{{cite web|url=http://www.couturefashionweek.com/angelique-monet-cannes-fashion-global-short-film-awards/|title=Angelique Monet Set to Perform at Cannes Fashion and Global Short Film Awards Gala  |publisher=[[Couture Fashion Week]]|accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> act on [[Theatre Row (New York City)|Theatre Row]] Off- Broadway in a one-woman show titled “Multiple Me”.

She is the owner of Greta Joanne Entertainment<ref>{{{cite web|url=https://filmmakermagazine.com/24467-cannes-the-producers-network-and-short-film-corner/|title=Cannes: The Producers Network And Short Film Corner  |publisher=[[Film Maker Magazine]]|accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref>

Her independent film, ''Deceptive''<ref>{{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1466458/|title=IMDB FILM FULL CAST AND CREW   |publisher=[[IMDB|Internet Movie Database]]|accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref> in which she produced, wrote, and starred premiered at the ''Marche du Film'' during ''[[Cannes Film Festival|Festival de Cannes]]'' (2010). The film also was theatrical released in New York.

Monet is the founder of the former Hamptons Black International Film Festival<ref>{{{cite web|url=https://ohgeeproductions.wordpress.com/2011/09/16/hamptons-black-international-film-festival-opening-night/|title=Hamptons Black International Film Festival Opening Night  |publisher=[[Oh Gee Productions]]|accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref>set in [[The Hamptons]]. She then went on to found the Artisan Festival International: World Peace Initiative.<ref>{{{cite web|url=https://www.filmfestivals.com/blog/cannes_market_dailies/afi_world_peace_initiative_in_cannes_awards/|title=AFI World Peace Initiative in Cannes Awards |publisher=[[Film Festivals]]|accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref>
