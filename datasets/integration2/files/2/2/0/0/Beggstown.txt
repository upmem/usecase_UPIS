'''Beggstown''' is a [[townland]] in [[County Westmeath]], [[Ireland]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Beggstown Townland, Co. Westmeath|url=https://www.townlands.ie/westmeath/fartullagh/kilbride/carrick/beggstown/|website=www.townlands.ie|access-date=2020-05-01}}</ref> The townland is located in the [[Civil parishes in Ireland|civil parish]] of [[Kilbride, County Westmeath (civil parish)|Kilbride]]. The [[R400 road (Ireland)|R400 regional road]] runs through the middle of the area. The townland is to the south of [[Mullingar]], and the north of [[Rochfortbridge]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Beggstown|url=https://www.logainm.ie/en/51542|website=Logainm.ie|language=en|access-date=2020-05-01}}</ref>
