'''Hamid Khozouie Abyane''' (born 1963) is an [[Iranian peoples|Iranian]] [[cinematographer]]. He is one of the most decorated cinematographers in contemporary [[Iranian cinema]], winning multiple [[Simorgh awards]] for his work.<ref>[https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0451932/bio?ref_=nm_ov_bio_sm Bio]</ref>
