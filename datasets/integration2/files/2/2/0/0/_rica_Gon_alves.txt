'''Èrica Gonçalves Do Canto''' (born 9 May 2002) is an Andorran [[women's association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for Andorran-based Spanish Women's First Division of Catalonia club ENFAF and the [[Andorra women's national football team|Andorra women's national team]].

==International career==
Gonçalves capped for Andorra at senior level in a 0–2 [[Exhibition game|friendly]] loss to [[Luxembourg women's national football team|Luxembourg]] on 10 November 2018.<ref>{{YouTube|id=kbnR1A2MBtg|title=Andorra vs Luxemburg}}</ref>
