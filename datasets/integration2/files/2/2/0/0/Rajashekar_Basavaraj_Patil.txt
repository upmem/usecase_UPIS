'''Rajashekar Basavaraj Patil''' is an Indian assemblyman who was the Minister of State for Mines and Geology of Karnataka from 23 May 2018 to 23 July 2019 and Member of Karnataka Legislative Assembly from Humnabad since from 2008.<ref>{{cite web |title=125 Humnabad RAJASHEKAR BASAVARAJ PATIL ರಾಜಶೇಖರ್‌ ಬಸವರಾಜ್‌ ಪಾಟೀಲ್‌ |url=http://www.kla.kar.nic.in/assembly/member/members.htm |website=www.kla.kar.nic.in |accessdate=1 May 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Humnabad Election Results 2018 Live Updates: Congress' Rajshekhar Basavaraj Patil Wins |url=https://www.news18.com/news/politics/humnabad-election-results-2018-live-updates-congress-rajshekhar-basavaraj-patil-wins-1748529.html |accessdate=1 May 2020 |publisher=www.news18.com}}</ref>

==Political career==
*[[2008 Karnataka Legislative Assembly election|2018]] MLA Humanabad Vidhana Sabha constituency as INC candidate.
*2018 Minister Mines and Zoology from Commerce and Industries Department in INC-JDS  coalition government.<ref>{{cite news |title=Karnataka Government: List of Ministers With Portfolio in H D Kumaraswamy Cabinet |url=https://www.india.com/news/india/karnataka-government-list-of-ministers-with-portfolio-in-hd-kumaraswamy-cabinet-3100260/ |accessdate=1 May 2020 |publisher=www.india.com}}</ref>
*[[2013 Karnataka Legislative Assembly election|2013 - 2018]] MLA Humnabad  Vidhana Sabha  constituency as INC candidate.
*[[2008 Karnataka Legislative Assembly election|2008 - 2013]] MLA Humnabad  Vidhana Sabha  constituency as INC candidate.
