The '''Singapore Lacrosse Association (SLA)''' is the organization that focuses on the growth of lacrosse development in Singapore. Formed in October 2012, the SLA became the 47th member of the [[Federation of International Lacrosse]] on 30 July 2013.<ref>{{cite journal|title=Singapore becomes FIL's 47th member nation|url=http://www.laxmagazine.com/international/men/2012-13/news/080713_singapore_becomes_fil_member_nation|journal=[[Lacrosse Magazine]]|publisher=[[US Lacrosse]]|date=7 August 2013|accessdate=16 February 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150216122246/http://www.laxmagazine.com/international/men/2012-13/news/080713_singapore_becomes_fil_member_nation|archive-date=16 February 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>
