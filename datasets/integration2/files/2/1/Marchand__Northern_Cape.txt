'''Marchand''' is a town in [[ZF Mgcawu District Municipality]] in the [[Northern Cape]] province of [[South Africa]].

The town is located on the [[R359 road (South Africa)|R359 road]], on the way to the [[Augrabie Falls]].<ref name=satd>{{cite web|title=Marchand, Northern Cape|url=http://sa.travel-directory.co/1208/places/northern-cape/marchand|publisher=SA Travel Directory|accessdate=4 January 2014}}</ref>
