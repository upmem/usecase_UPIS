'''Dorcan''' is an area in the east of [[Swindon]], [[Wiltshire]], England, located close to the [[A419]] ({{coord|51.56|N|01.73|W|region:GB}}). <ref>[https://www.geograph.org.uk/browse.php?p=165875 Geograph]</ref>

The population of Dorcan electoral ward taken at the [[United Kingdom Census 2011|2011 census]] was 8,684.<ref>{{cite web|title=Dorcan ward - 2011 Census|url=http://neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=6501459&c=dorcan&d=14&e=62&g=6393248&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=1&s=1439805172101&enc=1|website=Neighbourhood Statistics|publisher=Office for National Statistics|accessdate=15 August 2015}}</ref>

Taking its name from the nearby ''Dorkerne'' (now ''Dorcan'') brook,<ref name="dorcanname">{{Cite web|url=http://www.romanmap.com/htm/names/Nythe%20Farm.htm|title=Duroconovio Wanborough, Wiltshire|accessdate=2006-12-19|work=The Roman Map of Britain|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070102094612/http://www.romanmap.com/htm/names/Nythe%20Farm.htm|archivedate=2007-01-02}}</ref> Dorcan is within the civil parish of [[Nythe, Eldene and Liden]], and is bounded to the north by [[Covingham]] parish.  It is the site of the Dorcan Industrial Estate which includes the UK headquarters of [[Tyco International|Tyco Electronics]] and offices of [[Npower (UK)]], Equiinet, MMG MagDev, Romec, [[House of Fraser]] (information systems) and a [[Royal Mail]] [[sorting office]]. The pharmaceutical company [[Patheon]] also has a large facility here.

[[The Dorcan Academy]], a secondary school, is also in this area.
