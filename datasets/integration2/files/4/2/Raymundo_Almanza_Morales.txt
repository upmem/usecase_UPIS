'''Raymundo Almanza Morales''' (alias ''El Gori III'') is a [[Mexican people|Mexican]] suspected drug lord and former high-ranking member of [[Los Zetas]], a criminal group based in [[Tamaulipas]], Mexico. Almanza Morales was in the Mexican Armed Forces prior to his involvement in organized crime. He was infantry soldier and was discharged on August 14, 1999, at his own request.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://libertad-oaxaca.info/cinco-de-los-37-mas-buscados-estuvieron-en-filas-del-ejercito/|title=Cinco de los 37 más buscados estuvieron en filas del Ejército|last=|first=|date=11 November 2012|work=Libertad Oaxaca|access-date=|url-status=live|language=Spanish}}</ref> He was captured on 22 May 2009 in [[Monterrey]], [[Nuevo Leon]], [[Mexico]].<ref>[http://www.eluniversal.com.mx/primera/33019.html ''Cae operador del cártel del Golfo en Monterrey: Raymundo Almanza Morales, alias "El Gori", uno de los principales lugartenientes del cártel del Golfo, fue detenido en Monterrey, Nuevo León, informó la Secretaría de la Defensa Nacional (Sedena).''] Nubia Maya. El Universal. 22 May 2009. Retrieved 28 February 2013.</ref>
